US Government: At Least 3 Dead in Los Angeles Police Training Center Explosion

The US Attorney General said that at least 3 people died in an "incident" at a police training center in Los Angeles. News reports say it was an accidental explosion.

AFP reported that US Attorney General Pam Bondi posted on X (social media) that "a terrible event seems to have happened, causing at least 3 deaths at a law enforcement training center." Investigators are at the site to "keep understanding what happened."

California Governor Gavin Newsom's office stated that he was told about the "reported explosion" and is "watching the situation closely."

Fox News and other US media reported that the event at the Biscailuz Training Center seems to be an accident. At least 3 police officers died.

The Los Angeles Times, citing unnamed sources, reported that the explosion happened when the training center's explosives team was moving explosives.

European Stocks Show Mixed Results After US Data Boost

After a week of going up because of good US economic news and company reports, European stock markets had mixed results today.

The FTSE 100 index in London went up by 19.48 points, or 0.22%. It closed at 8992.12 points.

The DAX index in Frankfurt went down by 81.42 points, or 0.33%. It closed at 24289.51 points.

The CAC 40 index in Paris went up a little by 0.67 points, or 0.01%. It closed at 7822.67 points.

Typhoon Wipha Gets A Little Stronger, Heavy Rain Warning for 15 Counties

Taiwan's weather agency gave the latest typhoon warning this evening. Mild Typhoon Wipha has gotten a little stronger. Because of the outer parts of the typhoon, there will be strong rain for short times tonight and tomorrow. The weather agency also warned about heavy rain for 15 counties and cities.

The weather agency said that at 11 PM tonight, the middle of Wipha was about 280 kilometers south of Eluanbi. It is moving northwest at 22 kilometers per hour. The area with strong winds (level 7) is about 100 kilometers wide.

The weather agency said Wipha became a bit stronger in the last 3 hours. Its strong wind area is slowly moving into the Bashi Channel. It will be dangerous for the Bashi Channel, the southern Taiwan Strait, and the waters around Dongsha Island. The typhoon is expected to get a little stronger, and its strong wind area might get a little bigger.

The weather agency warned 15 counties and cities about heavy rain. Because of the typhoon's outer parts, there will be short periods of heavy rain. From tonight to tomorrow, there will be local heavy rain or very heavy rain in the mountains of Hualien County and Taitung County.

Hengchun Peninsula, Yilan County mountains, Nantou County mountains, Kaohsiung City mountains, Pingtung County mountains, and Hualien County and Taitung County (including Orchid Island and Green Island) will have local heavy rain or very heavy rain. Keelung's north coast, Yilan, southern areas, and the mountains in northern Taiwan, Taichung, and Chiayi will have local heavy rain. Hualien and Taitung might have very heavy rain.
