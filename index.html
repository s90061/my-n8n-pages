Cheng Li-wen, a Former DPP Member, Becomes KMT Chairperson

Former KMT legislator Cheng Li-wen has become the new chairperson of the Kuomintang (KMT) party. She was a surprise winner. She is known for her strong and brave style, which helped her win. She defeated other candidates like Hau Lung-pin, a former Taipei mayor, and Lo Chih-chiang, a KMT legislator.

Cheng Li-wen is the second woman to become KMT chairperson by direct vote from party members. The first was Hung Hsiu-chu.

Cheng Li-wen was born in Yunlin and grew up in Tainan. She studied law at National Taiwan University. During her time there, she took part in the Wild Lily student movement. After university, she joined the Democratic Progressive Party (DPP). She worked as a National Assembly member and a leader in the DPP Youth Department. But in 2002, she left the DPP. This was after she talked about the "Tu Hsing-che ear-licking case" and the DPP stopped her party rights.

After leaving the DPP, Cheng Li-wen stayed out of politics for some time. In 2004, she hosted a big protest for the Pan-Blue group against unfair presidential election results. This showed she supported the KMT side. Later that year, she ran for legislator in Kaohsiung but did not win.

However, Cheng Li-wen is very good at speaking and explaining things clearly. Lien Chan, who was the KMT chairperson at the time, noticed her skills. In 2005, she was asked to join the KMT. She then became the head of the KMT's Culture and Communications Committee and a spokesperson. In the 2008 election, she became a legislator. But in 2012, she tried to become a regional legislator in Taichung and lost to a DPP legislator.

Cheng Li-wen was brave to run in difficult election areas. This made her seem like a fighter, which is rare for the KMT. After losing the election, Chen Chun, who was the Premier at the time, asked her to be the spokesperson for the Executive Yuan. She was highly trusted.

In 2014, Cheng Li-wen left her job as spokesperson. She then worked in media and hosted TV shows. In the 2020 election, Wu Den-yih, the KMT chairperson at the time, chose her to be a legislator again. She returned to the Legislative Yuan and later became the KMT party whip.

During this time, the world had the COVID-19 pandemic. As the party whip, Cheng Li-wen was a strong voice for the KMT. She asked tough questions and fought hard for the party.

In this KMT chairperson election, Cheng Li-wen was a surprise candidate. She visited many local communities and showed a fighting spirit that was different from traditional KMT politicians. Many people in the military, government, and education fields supported her. She finally beat her opponents and became the party chairperson.

Cheng Li-wen is now the KMT chairperson, but she did not come from a traditional KMT background. The KMT is a complex party with many local powers. She also needs to work carefully with the Taiwan People's Party. Her challenge has just begun to help the KMT win back power in 2028.

Chunghwa Telecom Sponsors Taiwan Series; Chairman Chien Chih-cheng Throws a Good First Pitch

Chunghwa Telecom supports baseball in Taiwan. They are sponsoring the 2025 CPBL Chunghwa Telecom Taiwan Series again. Today, the company's chairman, Chien Chih-cheng, was invited to throw the first pitch. He threw a good pitch in front of 40,000 fans.

The Taiwan Series of the Chinese Professional Baseball League started at the Taipei Dome. The CTBC Brothers team is playing against the Rakuten Monkeys team. They will play up to 7 games to decide the champion. The teams said that 40,000 people came to watch, which is the same as the highest number of people for a first championship game. Chunghwa Telecom is the main sponsor of the 2025 CPBL Chunghwa Telecom Taiwan Series, and Mr. Chien was asked to throw the first pitch.

Chunghwa Telecom said that their platforms, MOD and Hami Video CPBLTV, are showing all the Taiwan Series games. This way, fans who cannot come to the stadium can still watch all the action. They can see who wins the championship and feel the excitement of Taiwan's top baseball event.

Also, Chunghwa Telecom's platforms, MOD and Hami Video, show many sports games all year. These include the MLB playoffs from America, HBL high school basketball, TPVL professional volleyball, BWF world badminton, and WTT world table tennis.

In addition to sports, movies and TV shows funded by Chunghwa Telecom also did well. They got 37 nominations for the Golden Bell Awards. For example, their main TV drama "The Outlaw Doctor" received 15 nominations, including for Best Drama and Best Director for a Drama.

Red Alert for Yanziqiao Landslide Lake; Some Areas in Xiulin Township Stop Work and School on October 19

There is a red alert for the landslide lake at Yanziqiao in the Liwu River. Because of this, the Hsiulin Township Office in Hualien County has announced that some areas will stop work and school tomorrow, October 19. These areas include the bigger Tianhsiang region (Guanyuan, Luoshao, Hsipao, and Tianhsiang), the Minle Community (neighborhood 9) in Fushi Village, and the Minyou Community (neighborhoods 1 to 3) in Hsiulin Village.

Authorities are still watching the landslide lake in Liwu River. This morning, October 18, at 6 AM, the water in the Liwu River had risen. It reached the top of the west entrance of the Jinheng Tunnel. The tunnel is now being used to let some water flow through. The Forest and Nature Conservation Agency and the Highway Bureau are working together. They will use machines on the old Provincial Highway 8 to remove some of the earth and rocks there.
