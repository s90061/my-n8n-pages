Lin Chih-Sheng's Retirement Games: Taipei 101 Lights Up to Honor a Legend

The Wei Chuan Dragons baseball team started special games today for Lin Chih-Sheng's retirement. Taipei 101 will also light up for him from the evening of September 5th to 7th. The famous building will use its lights to honor "Big Brother" Lin, who played baseball for over 20 years.

For three days, starting today, the Dragons are holding the "31CONIC Lin Chih-Sheng One Hit" retirement games for "Big Brother" Lin at the Taipei Dome. The team said in a press release that Taipei 101 will turn on special lights from 6:30 PM to 10:00 PM on September 5th to 7th to show respect to Lin Chih-Sheng.

The Dragons' press release also stated that Taipei 101 has always cared about sports and culture. They see sportsmanship as a very important part of their company's values. This special lighting shows not only the company's support for the games but also its respect for athletes who always try to improve themselves. Lin Chih-Sheng's strong effort, improvements, and determination on the field are like Taipei 101's international view and its constant desire to be the best. This shows that "true success is not just reaching the top, but always doing better than before."

The Wei Chuan Dragons team said that Taipei 101 is not just a famous building in the world, but it also stands for being brave to face challenges and wanting to be excellent. They are very happy about this light show because it connects a top symbol with a baseball icon. Also, fans' respect for the player can go up into the night sky through the lights, becoming a shining glory in the heavens. They hope everyone who looks up can feel the power that comes from sports and remember the legend Lin Chih-Sheng created with his strong will to win.

The Dragons said that Lin Chih-Sheng's retirement games will have their last two matches on September 6th and 7th. People are welcome to come and support the Chinese Professional Baseball League legend, Lin Chih-Sheng. After leaving the Taipei Dome, if you look at Taipei 101, you will see the light of glory shining in the sky. This is to show the deepest respect for "Big Brother Forever."

New Taipei City Shisizhang Station Metro Development Project Finished Choosing, Will Bring in 98.5 Billion NTD Investment

The New Taipei City Department of Rapid Transit Systems announced today that the biggest metro development project in Taiwan has finished choosing a company. This project is for the Shisizhang Station and South Maintenance Depot on the New Taipei Circular Line. They expect private companies to invest about 98.5 billion New Taiwan Dollars, and the developed area will be more than 250,000 ping (a unit of area).

The Department of Rapid Transit Systems said in a press release that the largest metro development project in Taiwan, "New Taipei Circular Line Shisizhang Station and South Maintenance Depot Metro Development Project," has finished its selection process. A team formed by Honghui, Aishanlin Construction Development, and Jiasanlin Construction won the bid. After they set the final conditions, the winning team will be asked to sign an blank investment contract.

The Department of Rapid Transit Systems explained that the Shisizhang Station and South Maintenance Depot development area is about 14.3 hectares. They expect private investment to be around 98.5 billion New Taiwan Dollars, and the developed area will be over 250,000 ping. The plan includes large shopping malls, office spaces, and apartment buildings. It also has the advantage of two metro lines, the New Taipei Circular Line and the Ankeng Light Rail, making it easy to reach Ankeng, Xindian, Zhonghe, Banqiao, and Xinzhuang areas quickly.

In addition, the development area is very close to the finished Yangbei land readjustment area and the Shisizhang Unit B land readjustment area, which started construction last month. Through this total development, it is expected to become the new central area of Southern New Taipei City, creating a brand new look for the Xindian District.
