Low-Pressure Area Brings Heavy Rain to Hualien and Taitung

The Central Weather Bureau has given a special warning for heavy rain. A low-pressure area is causing strong, short bursts of rain. Today, Hualien County and Taitung County might have heavy rain or very heavy rain. Green Island, Orchid Island, and Hengchun Peninsula also have a chance of heavy rain.

The Central Weather Bureau reminds people to be careful of lightning, strong winds, and rising river levels. Be careful of flooding in low-lying areas. Because it has been raining for many days, be careful of landslides, falling rocks, and mudslides in mountain areas. Northern and northeastern parts of Taiwan might also get some heavy rain.

No One Hurt in Chiayi Factory Accident, TSMC Asks Contractors to Make Improvements

Taipei, July 20 (CNA) TSMC (2330), a leading chipmaker, reported an accident today at its Chiayi factory site. A flatbed truck overturned, but luckily no one was hurt. TSMC said in the evening that the construction work stopped immediately. The site has now been cleaned up. TSMC will continue to ask its main contractors and sub-contractors to make needed improvements.

The Southern Occupational Safety and Health Center of the Ministry of Labor said that at noon today, they received a report about a truck overturning at the TSMC Chiayi factory site. A flatbed truck carrying many steel beams was driving on the factory road. It might have been too heavy. When it turned, it accidentally flipped over. Luckily, no one was injured. The center will send staff to check the site and ask for improvements.

TSMC said in the evening that at noon on July 20, a flatbed truck accidentally overturned while moving in the construction area given to a contractor at TSMC's Southern Taiwan Science Park. Luckily, no one was hurt at the scene.

TSMC said that the construction work stopped immediately. The main contractor also told the authorities as required. The reason for the accident is still being checked. The site has now been cleaned up.

TSMC stated that it will continue to ask its main contractors and sub-contractors to make necessary improvements.

South China Morning Post: Trump and Xi Might Meet Before or During APEC Summit

Seoul, July 20 (CNA) The "South China Morning Post" in Hong Kong reported that several sources said the APEC summit in Korea this year could be the best chance for Chinese President Xi Jinping and U.S. President Donald Trump to meet face-to-face this year.

The Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation (APEC) summit will be held in Gyeongju, South Korea, from October 30 to November 1 this year. These sources said that Trump might visit China before the summit or meet with Xi Jinping during the APEC summit.

Korean media reported that Xi Jinping plans to attend this APEC summit. However, it is still not known if Trump will be there.
