Asian Soft Tennis Championship: Yu Kai-wen and Huang Shih-yuan Win Mixed Doubles Gold

At the Asian Soft Tennis Championship in Korea, Yu Kai-wen and Huang Shih-yuan won a gold medal in mixed doubles today. They beat a Japanese team 5-2 in the final match. This is the first gold medal for Taiwan's soft tennis team in 17 years. The team is now looking forward to the 2026 Nagoya Asian Games.

The 9th Asian Soft Tennis Championship is happening in Mungyeong, Korea. Yesterday, Chen Po-yi won a silver medal in men's singles for Taiwan. Today, Yu Kai-wen and Huang Shih-yuan worked together in mixed doubles to win another medal. They brought the team its first Asian Championship gold in 17 years. Sports Minister Lee Yang quickly sent a message to congratulate them. He hopes the Taiwanese soft tennis players will keep trying their best to win more honors.

Yu Kai-wen and Huang Shih-yuan played very well in the mixed doubles competition. In the semifinal match, they played against a Japanese team that included Uematsu Toshitaka, who has won three World Championship gold medals. But Yu Kai-wen stopped Uematsu's strong shots, and Huang Shih-yuan played well from the back of the court. They won 5-2 and moved on to the gold medal match.

In the final, they played another Japanese team. Yu Kai-wen and Huang Shih-yuan continued their good teamwork. They quickly got a 3-0 lead. Even when the other team tried to catch up, the Taiwanese team stayed strong in the important 6th game. They broke the tie and won the match 5-2. This was Taiwan's first gold medal at this Asian Soft Tennis Championship.

Zhu Wen-ching, the chairman of the Soft Tennis Association, said that this mixed doubles gold medal made the whole team feel very happy. It also ended Taiwan's nearly 17-year wait for a win at the Asian Championship. He hopes that the new generation of players will do even better and aim for the highest honors at the 2026 Nagoya Asian Games.

Taipei Auto Show Starts on December 31, Focusing on Electric and Smart Green Energy

The Taipei New Car and New Energy Vehicle Show will start on December 31. This event will show the trend of cars becoming hybrid and electric. Many car brands from Taiwan and other countries will show fancy, fast, electric, and old-style cars. People who visit can try smart technology and learn about new ideas in smart green energy.

The Taipei Auto Agents Association is putting on the show. It will happen from December 31 to January 4, 2026. The association said today that big car brands will join. They will show cars that are hybrid (HEV/PHEV) and fully electric (BEV). They will focus on better technology, cars that can drive longer distances, and more places to charge electric cars.

The association also said that many car brands will come to this car show. There will be fancy cars, fast cars, electric cars, and classic cars. Many new car models will be shown for the very first time. These new cars will show the newest designs, technology, and driving fun.

Also, there will be a special area called the "Mobility Zone" at the car show. In this area, you can see things like car chargers, useful car items, camping cars, and special films for car windows. This will help visitors learn about new ways to travel.

The association said that during the show, visitors can also try smart driving, new charging systems, ways to make less carbon pollution, cars that connect to the internet, and car software updates that happen online. The event will also have talks about clean energy for travel. These talks will discuss new ways to make less carbon, how the government can help, and how car companies are changing. This will help visitors understand the future of smart green energy and changes in the car industry.

Jimmy Kimmel's Show Is Stopped After He Talks About Kirk Shooting; Hollywood Shows Support

The TV channel ABC stopped Jimmy Kimmel's late-night talk show. This happened after he talked about the Charlie Kirk shooting and because the Trump government put pressure on them. Today, people in Hollywood are supporting Kimmel and criticizing the White House for trying to stop free speech.

The TV network, which is owned by Walt Disney, said that "Jimmy Kimmel Live!" will not be shown for an unknown amount of time. This is because at least one local TV station refused to show it. Also, the main communication office said they would investigate Kimmel's comments.

Reuters reported that Jimmy Kimmel often criticized Trump. On his show on September 15, he talked about the death of Charlie Kirk, who was a conservative online personality. Kimmel said that Kirk's friends were using his death "to get political advantages."

The Writers Guild and the Actors Guild said that stopping Kimmel's show because of what he said is against the right to speak freely, which is protected by the country's main law. They said that ABC should not give in to pressure from the government.

This is another important event. Before this, other people like journalists, professors, teachers, and company workers were also criticized for what they said about Kirk's death.

Kirk died last week in Utah. He was 31 years old. The police have charged a 22-year-old person as a suspect, but they do not yet know why the shooting happened.
