Pop Queen Taylor Swift Announces Engagement to Star Athlete Boyfriend on Social Media

American pop singer Taylor Swift has some happy news. Today, she announced on social media that she is engaged to Travis Kelce. He is a famous American football player for the NFL Chiefs team. They have been together for more than two years.

Reuters reported that they both wrote on an Instagram post: "Your English teacher and your sports teacher are getting married." The post also had a picture of Kelce asking Swift to marry him.

Less than 20 minutes after they posted the news, over 1.8 million fans liked it. The NFL also sent them their good wishes.

Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce have been a couple for over two years. They started dating after Taylor Swift performed at the Chiefs' home stadium, Arrowhead Stadium, in 2023. But Travis Kelce had shown interest in Taylor Swift back in 2016.

French Political Instability Adds Market Uncertainty, European Stocks Close Lower

Investors are worried about new political problems in France. This has made the market less sure about the future. Because of this, the three main European stock markets closed lower today. Bank stocks in Paris also went down.

The FTSE 100 index in London fell by 55.60 points, which is 0.60%. It closed at 9265.80 points.

The DAX index in Frankfurt went down by 120.25 points, which is 0.50%. It closed at 24152.87 points.

The CAC 40 index in Paris dropped a lot, by 133.23 points or 1.70%. It closed at 7709.81 points.

Zhubei Air Smells Like Burning Plastic, Environmental Protection Bureau Investigates Suspected Odor Leak

The Hsinchu County Environmental Protection Bureau said tonight that the air in many parts of Zhubei City smells like burning plastic. They first thought it was not from burning things outside. Instead, they think some smelly material has leaked out. They will keep looking for where the pollution is coming from.

The Hsinchu County Environmental Protection Bureau said in a written statement that they have checked many areas. These include parts of Guangming 6th Road, Zhuangjing North and South Roads, Jiafeng North and South Roads, around the High-Speed Rail Station, parts of Dongxing Road, and Shixing Road. They have not found where the pollution might be coming from yet.

The bureau explained that they looked at results from their sensors. The sensor results first showed that it was probably not outdoor burning. It seems more like a bad smell has leaked. However, the sensors did not show clear changes in time or place. This means they cannot use the sensor results to find the source of the pollution right now. They will continue to investigate to find out more.
