FBI: DNA at scene of US conservative influencer Kirk's murder matches suspect

The FBI director, Kash Patel, said today that DNA found at the place where American conservative influencer Charlie Kirk was murdered matches the suspect, Tyler Robinson.

News reports say that Robinson, who is 22, was caught on September 12th after police looked for him for 33 hours. He will be charged with murder later this week.

Kirk was shot and killed on September 10th at a university in Utah during a speech. He started "Turning Point USA," a well-known group for young conservative people.

Police said the suspect used a sniper rifle from a rooftop. He fired one shot that hit Kirk in the neck.

Patel said on Fox News this morning, "Today I can tell you that DNA on a towel wrapped around the gun and on a screwdriver found at the crime scene matches the DNA of the suspect we have arrested."

Patel also said that Robinson is believed to have written a note before the crime.

Patel explained, the note "basically said... 'I have a chance to get rid of Kirk, and I will take it.' This note was written before the shooting."

He added that the note was left at the suspect's home.

Patel said, "Even if the note was destroyed, we still found evidence from it."

Trump goes to UK for visit; US and UK will announce over $10 billion agreement

High-ranking American officials said today that the United States and the United Kingdom plan to announce economic agreements worth over 10 billion US dollars. This will happen during US President Donald Trump's official visit this week.

Reuters reported that American officials said in a phone briefing that the two governments plan to sign a trade agreement with three main parts. These parts include creating a new technology partnership to make both countries' tech industries stronger, working together on peaceful nuclear energy, and improving cooperation on defense technology.

Officials said that many leaders from American technology companies are expected to be at this official visit.

Trump and the First Lady, Melania Trump, will arrive tomorrow evening. On the 17th, they will meet with King Charles III and Queen Camilla at Windsor Castle. They will also go to a special dinner there that evening.

On the 18th, Trump will meet with British Prime Minister Keir Starmer. They plan to hold a joint press conference. After that, Trump will return to Washington D.C. on the evening of the 18th.

Trump is the first US President to be invited twice to a state dinner by the UK. The first time was in 2019, during his first term as president.

American officials said that the new agreements will focus on working together on the economy. They expect the total value to be at least 10 billion US dollars.

One American official said, "This visit gives the President a chance to make the relationship stronger with this very close partner and friend. It also helps both sides with their shared goals in economy and foreign policy."
