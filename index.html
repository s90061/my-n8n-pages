Singapore's Important Computer Systems Attacked; Experts Think China is Involved

(Central News Agency, Singapore, July 19th) Singapore said yesterday that its important computer systems were attacked. Security experts believe a spy group linked to China carried out the cyberattack. The government is now dealing with this attack.

Reuters reported that K. Shanmugam, Singapore's Minister for National Security and Home Affairs, spoke about the attack. He said, "UNC3886 is a serious danger to us. It could harm our country's safety. This group attacks important strategic places and systems that provide necessary services."

Mr. Shanmugan did not share many details about the attack or what happened because of security reasons.

Mandiant, a security company owned by Google, said that UNC3886 is "a spy group connected to China." This group has attacked defense, technology, and communication companies in the United States and Asia before.

Beijing has always said that it does not do any cyber spying.

Earlier this week, Reuters also reported that hackers linked to China have been trying to spy on Taiwan's computer chip industry and people who study investments.

Researchers from Proofpoint, a cybersecurity company, said that at least three different groups from China carried out these hacker attacks. These attacks were mostly from March to June this year, and some might still be happening.

Weather Agency Warns of Heavy Rain in Hualien and Taitung Mountains

(Central News Agency, Taipei, July 19th) The Central Weather Agency has issued a special warning for heavy rain. The outer part of a typhoon is causing short periods of strong rain. Today, the mountain areas of Hualien County and Taitung County will have heavy rain or even very heavy rain. Other areas like Hengchun Peninsula, mountain areas in Yilan, Nantou, Kaohsiung, and Pingtung counties, as well as Hualien County and Taitung County (including Lanyu and Green Island), might have local heavy rain or very heavy rain. The north coast of Keelung, Yilan, southern areas, and mountain areas in northern Taiwan, Taichung, and Chiayi might also have local heavy rain. Please be careful of lightning, strong winds, and rivers rising quickly. In mountain areas, please watch out for landslides, falling rocks, and mudslides. In low-lying areas, please be careful of flooding. The areas with very heavy rain are Hualien County and Taitung County.

US Government: At Least 3 People Died in Los Angeles Police Training Center Explosion

(Central News Agency, Los Angeles, July 18th) The US Attorney General said that at least three people died in an "incident" at a police training center in Los Angeles. Media reports said there was an accidental explosion at the time.

Agence France-Presse reported that US Attorney General Pam Bondi wrote on social media platform X that "a terrible event seems to have happened, causing at least three deaths at a law enforcement training center." She added that investigators are still at the place trying to understand what happened.

The office of California Governor Gavin Newsom said that he has been told about the "reported explosion." He is "watching the situation closely."

Fox News and other US media reported that the event at the Biscailuz Training Center seemed to be an accident. At least three police officers died.

The Los Angeles Times, quoting unnamed sources, reported that the explosion happened while the training center's explosives team was moving explosive materials.
