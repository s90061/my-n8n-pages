CDMO Business Grows, Bora Pharmaceuticals Earns NT$19.5 EPS in First Half of Year

Bora Pharmaceuticals shared its financial report for the first half of the year. Their sales reached 9.347 billion New Taiwan Dollars. Thanks to the growth in their CDMO business, the company's net profit was 2.016 billion New Taiwan Dollars, which is 11.9% more than last year. Their earnings per share (EPS) were 19.5 New Taiwan Dollars, almost earning two times their stock value.

Bora Pharmaceuticals announced today that their CDMO (Contract Development and Manufacturing Organization) business grew by 69.5% in the first half of this year, reaching over 5.01 billion New Taiwan Dollars. This growth was due to more production capacity and new types of products. However, during a period of merging, there were delays in production at their sterile injection factories in Maryland, USA, and Zhunan, Taiwan. This slowed things down. But this problem got better in June, and delayed orders will be filled soon. The company's orders look good for the future, and there is a steady need for their services.

Bora Pharmaceuticals said they continue to invest more in their CDMO business around the world. Recently, their factory in Canada added a new production line for filling high-performance skin medicines. This will help them produce these orders faster, in larger amounts, and more flexibly. This new line will help the Canadian factory grow.

The Flex-Pro sterile injection production line in Maryland, USA, is expected to start working in the third quarter of this year. From 2026, the automatic AST isolated filling systems will begin to operate. Also, in the first half of this year, clients at their Maple Grove factory in Minnesota, USA, became stronger, long-term partners. Many drug companies are now signing contracts because they want to use the benefits of making products in the USA.

Sheng Pao-hsi, the chairman of Bora Pharmaceuticals, said that their CDMO business has gained 8 new clients this year. Most of these new clients are handled by the Maple Grove factory, which focuses on manufacturing in North America. This year, new orders worth 138 million US dollars have been received. Most of these new orders are for the Canadian and sterile product factories. For large molecule CDMO, the San Diego factory, which was part of the merger with TaiFu Biologics, has started operations. Many American drug companies that are in the later stages of development have asked about the plan to increase production capacity to 2000 liters.

Taiwan Life Insurance Moves into Taoyuan Aerotropolis, Plans to Invest Over NT$50 Billion

On September 25 last year, the Taoyuan City Government announced that Taiwan Life Insurance won the bid for a piece of land in the Taoyuan Aerotropolis Priority Industrial Zone I. They paid 13.7 billion New Taiwan Dollars for it. Today, Taiwan Life Insurance said that their total investment for the whole project will be more than 50 billion New Taiwan Dollars. They plan to build a multi-purpose sports center, hotels, and logistics facilities. They hope this project will become a new landmark in the area.

The Taoyuan City Government announced on September 25 last year that Taiwan Life Insurance won the bid for the largest piece of land in the Taoyuan Aerotropolis Priority Industrial Zone I. They won it with a bid of 13,749,796,800 New Taiwan Dollars.

Taiwan Life Insurance released an important update today. They have formed a joint company with Evergreen International Storage & Transport Corporation. This new company will invest in the land in Aerotropolis Zone I. Both sides have signed an agreement to work together and create this new company.

Taiwan Life Insurance explained that this project is located within the Taoyuan Aerotropolis, which is an important area in a major national development plan. It has good potential for growth. The land for this project is large. They hope to create a new landmark for Taoyuan, making it a key multi-purpose park with many uses.

Taiwan Life Insurance said they will follow the Taoyuan City Government's bid instructions and review comments. They will build a multi-purpose sports center that meets international competition standards. There will also be shops and hotels connected to it to help the area grow. Additionally, they will build new logistics and office buildings based on local market and industry needs. The total investment for this project is expected to be over 50 billion New Taiwan Dollars.

Taiwan Life Insurance also mentioned that the multi-purpose sports center will have about 8,000 seats. It can be used for basketball games and other types of sports events.
