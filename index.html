Elderly Man in Taichung's Shigang Area Unhurt After Car Rolls Off Road

An 87-year-old man named Mr. Chuang was driving his car yesterday near number 126, Shihgang Street, Shihgang Lane, in Taichung City's Shihgang District. He might not have known the road well, and the road was narrow. As he drove there, his car accidentally slipped off the side of the road and overturned. The driver was able to climb out of the car by himself and was not hurt.

Today, the Dongshi Police Station in Taichung City released a statement. It said that yesterday at about 9:29 AM, a small car at number 126, Shihgang Street, Shihgang Lane, in Shihgang District, slipped off the road and overturned. This happened because the road was narrow and the driver did not know the road well. The driver got out of the car on his own and was lucky not to be hurt.

Police did a first check at the scene. They found that the driver of the small car was 87-year-old Mr. Chuang. He was driving alone at the time of the accident. There were no traffic lights or signs, and the road markings were normal. Also, there was no construction happening. Mr. Chuang had not been drinking and was not injured. The police finished their work and recorded the details at the scene.

Wu Yung-hsin, the head of the Shihgang police office at Dongshi Police Station, reminded people that roads in the mountains are narrow and winding. Drivers should slow down when they are on roads they do not know well. They should also pay attention to the road conditions to avoid accidents.

Typhoon Podul Will Affect Taiwan Most From August 13th; Sea Warning Possible

The Weather Bureau has announced that a typhoon has formed. The tropical depression that was north-northeast of Guam became a mild typhoon at 2 AM. It is the 11th typhoon and is named Podul. After August 12, it will slowly get closer to the sea southeast of Taiwan. It will affect Taiwan most on August 13 and 14. A sea warning might be issued.

The Central Weather Bureau said that the tropical depression, which was north-northeast of Guam, became a mild typhoon named Podul at 2 AM. The center of the typhoon is 2660 kilometers east of Eluanbi. It is expected to move west.

A weather forecaster, Tseng Chao-cheng, told CNA reporters that Typhoon Podul will get closer to the sea southeast of Taiwan after August 12. The time it will affect Taiwan the most is on August 13 and 14. The eastern part of Taiwan will have a lot of rain.

Tseng Chao-cheng also reminded everyone that they need to watch if the typhoon moves closer to Taiwan or the Bashi Channel, because the effect on Taiwan will be different. If the typhoon gets very close to the sea east of Taiwan, a sea typhoon warning might be issued. But right now, there is still a lot of uncertainty about its path.
