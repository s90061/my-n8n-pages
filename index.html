Europe and US May Agree on Trade Deal, European Stocks Mostly Higher

(Central News Agency, London, July 24, combined foreign reports) Most major stock markets in Europe went up today. This happened because there is a growing hope that the European Union (EU) and the United States might reach a trade agreement.

The FTSE 100 index in London went up by 76.88 points, or 0.85%, closing at 9138.37 points.

The DAX index in Frankfurt went up by 55.11 points, or 0.23%, closing at 24295.93 points.

Taiwan Men's Basketball Loses Closely to Poland by 2 Points at Universiade, Misses Best Historical Record

(Central News Agency reporter Chen Jung-tsen, Taipei, July 24) Today, the Taiwan men's basketball team played a game for 9th to 12th place at the World University Games in Rhine-Ruhr, Germany. Even though Hsu Te-chi scored the most points for the team with 17 points and 5 assists, Taiwan still lost to Poland 87 to 89. This means they will not break their best record in team history.

At the last World University Games in Chengdu, China, the Taiwan men's basketball team got 11th place, which was their best record ever. This time, team captain You Ai-che, who plays overseas, led the team again, along with other Taiwanese professional basketball players like Lin Hsin-kuan and Chen Fan-po-yen. However, they only won one game in the group stage, so they could not get into the top 8. Luckily, after beating India by a large score yesterday, the Taiwan men's basketball team moved on to the games for 9th to 12th place.

Today, the Taiwan men's basketball team played against Poland. They wanted to win and get into the game for 9th or 10th place, which would be their best record ever. They started the first quarter well. But in the second quarter, they didn't shoot as well, and Taiwan was behind by 2 points at halftime.

After halftime, the game was still very close. The Taiwan team kept the score tight. At the start of the fourth quarter, they scored 9 points to Poland's 3, taking the lead again. Even though Poland caught up later, Li Yun-chieh and Sung Hsin-hao made three-point shots near the end of the game, helping Taiwan tie the score. This gave them a chance to play in overtime.

However, when they were behind by 2 points, Hsu Te-chi took the last shot but missed. So, the Taiwan men's basketball team lost to Poland. This means they just missed breaking their best record in team history. Next, they will play against Latvia for 11th place. If they lose that game, they will finish the World University Games in 12th place.

In this game, five players from the Taiwan men's basketball team scored ten or more points. Hsu Te-chi scored the most with 17 points, and also had 5 assists and 2 steals. The shooter Li Yun-chieh played for less than 12 minutes but scored 14 points very effectively, including 4 three-pointers. Captain You Ai-che showed his all-around skills, getting 10 points, 6 rebounds, 4 assists, and 3 steals.
