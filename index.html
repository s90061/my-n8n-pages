Taipower Worker Li Yuan-han Dies After Electric Shock; Chiayi Prosecutors Say It Was an Accident

Chiayi City, July 22 (CNA reporter Jiang Yi-ching) â€“ Li Yuan-han, a Taipower worker who was shocked by electricity, was saved for many days. His family decided to remove his life support this morning and donate his organs. Chiayi District Prosecutors Office checked his body and said that Li Yuan-han died from an accident. He had many high-voltage electrical injuries on his body from the high-voltage electrical accident. He eventually died because his brain did not get enough oxygen.

Typhoon Danas caused power outages in many areas of southern Taiwan. Li Yuan-han was fixing power lines when he was shocked by electricity and was in danger of dying. After many days of medical help, he did not get better. So, his family decided today to let Li Yuan-han pass away and donate his organs.

This afternoon, the Chiayi District Prosecutors Office checked his body. They decided that Li Yuan-han died because of a high-voltage electrical accident. This caused many high-voltage electrical injuries, burns on both arms and the front of his chest, muscle breakdown, and kidney failure. In the end, he died from a brain injury because he did not get enough oxygen. His death was an accident.

More than a hundred Taipower employees gathered at the Chiayi Power Distribution Center today. They cried as they said goodbye to Li Yuan-han. They shouted, "Yuan-han, your work is done, rest in peace."

Chiayi Mayor Huang Min-hui felt sad about Li Yuan-han's death. She said she remembered going to the hospital to support Li Yuan-han that day. His father shared photos of Li Yuan-han at work. She saw him smiling brightly, and his eyes showed great passion. This showed his love for his job and his deep feelings for his family.

Huang Min-hui said that Li Yuan-han's father had said, "A soldier cannot choose their battlefield." So, Li Yuan-han had worked hard for many days on the front line of power repairs. He did this just to protect this land.

Huang Min-hui stated that many people are sad about Li Yuan-han's passing. And his choice to donate organs shows how love continues. "Thank you, Yuan-han, we will always remember your work and your help in protecting this land." (Editor: Lin Shu-hui) 1140722

All the coaches and players worked hard. After three years, they won the national championship again. This is the 7th time in the team's history.

This shows the school's long-term effort in sports education. She also thanked the teachers, parents, and players for working together to achieve this proud moment.

Yang Cheng-hao and Jian Rui-chen won the Hitting award. Also, coaches You Shen-ping, Yang Zheng-an, and Xiao You-hong won the Best Coach award.

The sports budget for 2024 and 2025 is 30% higher than before. This money is used to improve school sports facilities, pay coaches for their hours, and train good athletes.
