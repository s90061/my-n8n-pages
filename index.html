Wei Chuan Starts 'Sports Science Nutrition Center' with National Sports University and Wei Chuan Dragons

Wei Chuan announced today that it has joined with National Taiwan Sport University and the professional baseball team Wei Chuan Dragons. They have opened a "Sports Science Nutrition Center" at Tianmu Baseball Stadium in Taipei. They also signed an agreement for this partnership. In the future, they will combine sports science research with athletes' real-life experience. The goal is to create nutrition products that better meet the needs of sports people. They want to offer these products to both professional athletes and the general public.

Wei Chuan sent out a press release today. It said that the "Sports Science Nutrition Center" was officially opened today at Tianmu Baseball Stadium. A signing ceremony for the three-way partnership with National Taiwan Sport University and the Wei Chuan Dragons team also took place.

Mr. Hsu Wen-fang, the chairman of Wei Chuan Company, Mr. Chiu Ping-kun, the president of National Taiwan Sport University, and Mr. Chien Pei-hsiang, the chairman of Wei Chuan Dragons, attended the ceremony. Ms. Hsu Yi-ju, who was the national team's nutritionist for the 2024 WBSC Premier12 baseball tournament and is the director of the Institute of Sports Science at National Taiwan Sport University, will be the main advisor. They have set up a group of sports nutrition advisors. This group will offer ideas for new products and support with scientific research.

Wei Chuan said they will build a new platform that connects food nutrition with sports science. They hope this will bring new energy to Taiwan's food industry.

During the opening ceremony, Wei Chuan Dragons players Gili Guli Kungkuan and Chiang Shao-hung were also invited. They talked with the nutrition team and shared their own nutrition plans and how they use them in games. Ms. Hsu Yi-ju said that athletes understand nutrition more and more. She added that making personal changes helps athletes keep their physical condition stable and recover faster.

Wei Chuan said that this partnership is not just for professional athletes. They also hope to help people who exercise a lot and everyday consumers in the future. They will do this through interviews and testing products. This will help make sports nutrition products more widely used.

Bus and Motorcycle Crash in Hsinchu City, Rider Dies Trapped Under Bus

A deadly traffic accident happened this afternoon in Hsinchu City. A bus was driving on Xuefu Road when, for unclear reasons, it crashed into a motorcycle. The motorcycle was being ridden by a 44-year-old man named Mr. Chang. Police and firefighters arrived at the scene and rescued Mr. Chang, who was trapped under the bus. However, he had no signs of life. The police will investigate to find out why the accident happened.

The Hsinchu City Fire Department said in a message that they received a call at 4:45 PM today. The call reported a car accident in front of 86 Xuefu Road. They immediately sent people to the location.

The Fire Department reported that when rescue workers arrived, they found Mr. Chang trapped under the bus. He was not breathing and had no heartbeat. More people and vehicles were quickly sent to help get him out. Mr. Chang died from his serious injuries. The police will investigate the exact cause of the accident.

AI Net-Zero: Dehchuan Starts Biodegradable Hot Melt Adhesive Line to Boost Green Materials Business

Changing to digital ways of working is happening in industries now. Artificial intelligence and reaching net-zero carbon emissions are two big challenges for governments and companies. These are important for Taiwan's future success. The Central News Agency's reporting team went to see how the government and different businesses are using AI to make their production better and to create new green ideas. They saw Taiwan's industries making progress again.

Dehchuan Enterprise spent five years getting ready. Today, they officially opened Asia's first "biodegradable hot melt adhesive production line" and a new research and development building called "Innovation Building" at their factory in Taoyuan.

Mr. Hsiao Hsiang-chih, the chairman of Dehchuan, said that in the first half of 2025, 86% of the company's main adhesive products will be made from green materials. Out of this, 24% will be new green materials. The company has brought together its global research, development, and management teams. They have worked together to create over 100 new green products and technologies.

Mr. Hsiao Hsiang-chih explained that their green products and solutions are used in many industries. These include the "GPS plastic-free stretch film" for reducing plastic and carbon, which is a sustainable solution. They also make glue for car parts in new energy vehicles, easy-to-remove hot melt glue, glues made from natural sources, low-smell and low-waste hot melt glue, and eco-friendly fire-resistant hot melt glue without halogens.

He said that because of serious problems like global warming, climate change, and the need for environmental protection, Dehchuan has been working on these issues. They started by promoting sustainable materials from natural sources. Now, they are focusing on net-zero carbon emissions, which is a very important topic. They are developing materials and solutions that are good for the environment, safe, and healthy.

Also, in 2021, Dehchuan worked with other companies that supply and use their products. They created the GPS Green Materials Strategic Cooperation Platform. Through different collaborations, they have built new technologies and partnerships. This helps industries become more eco-friendly and move towards the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).
