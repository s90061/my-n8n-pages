People See Many Locusts in New Taipei, Agricultural Bureau Says It's Normal for the Season

(Central News Agency Reporter Chao Min-Ya, New Taipei, August 1) People have recently reported seeing locusts in many parts of New Taipei. Today, the New Taipei City Government Agricultural Bureau said that experts told them locusts grow fast in August due to high temperatures. So, it is common to see them now. This is a natural event for the season. If people are worried, they can use pesticides at home to control them.

The Agricultural Bureau also sent a message. It said that a researcher from the Council of Agriculture's Taoyuan District Agricultural Research and Extension Station believes that the number of locusts will slowly go down when the weather gets cooler or it rains. People do not need to be scared.

Yesterday, some people posted photos on Threads, a social media website. They said they often saw locusts in Zhonghe and Yonghe districts in New Taipei recently. Other people commented that they also had a "bug problem." They reported similar situations in Xinzhuang, Tucheng, Shulin, Banqiao, Sanchong, Xindian, and Taishan districts.

US Job Data Is Weak, US Stock Market Falls A Lot, Dow Jones Drops 600 Points

(New York, Central News Agency, August 1, reported by foreign press) Today, the United States announced weak job numbers. This made investors more worried about the US economy. They were already worried about new taxes on goods from other countries. Because of this, the three main US stock indexes fell by at least 1.5% this morning. The Dow Jones index dropped more than 600 points.

AFP reported that the US Department of Labor announced today that only 73,000 new jobs were added in July. Also, the number of new jobs for May and June was much lower than first thought. After this news, the prices of US government bonds went down quickly. And the futures market now thinks the US central bank (Fed) will cut interest rates in September.

About 25 minutes after the US stock market opened today, the Dow Jones Industrial Average dropped 1.5% to 43477.54 points. The S&P 500 index fell 1.7% to 6231.49 points. The Nasdaq index dropped even more, 2.3% to 20644.16 points.

Regarding individual company stocks, Amazon, a large US technology company, released its future earnings predictions. These predictions made people worry that its big investments in Artificial Intelligence (AI) would hurt its money. So, its stock price fell by 7.0% this morning.

Slovenia Is First EU Country to Stop All Weapon Trade with Israel

(Ljubljana, Central News Agency, August 1, reported by foreign press) The government of Slovenia has announced that it will completely stop trading weapons with Israel. This is because of the war in the Gaza Strip. Slovenia says it is the first country in the European Union (EU) to do this.

AFP reported that the Slovenian government released a statement late last night. It said: "Slovenia is the first European country to stop importing, exporting, and moving weapons to Israel."

The statement also said that Slovenia acted "alone." This is because the EU "cannot take strong actions... due to disagreements and a lack of unity inside."

The Slovenian government's statement also mentioned that in the terrible Gaza war, "people are dying... because they are not getting enough help with food and medicine." It added that "taking action is the duty of every responsible country, even if it means going ahead of other countries."
