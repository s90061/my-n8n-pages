AI Hopeful Outlook Boosts Oracle Stock, US Stocks Mostly Up, S&P Reaches New High

People are feeling good about Artificial Intelligence (AI). This has made the price of Oracle, a big American software company, go up a lot. Also, people think the US Federal Reserve will lower interest rates. Because of these reasons, most major US stock markets finished higher today, and the S&P 500 reached its highest point ever.

The Dow Jones Industrial Average went down by 220.42 points, which is 0.48%. It closed at 45490.92 points.

The S&P 500 index went up a little, by 19.43 points or 0.30%. It closed at 6532.04 points.

The Nasdaq index went up by 6.57 points, which is 0.03%. It closed at 21886.06 points.

The Philadelphia Semiconductor Index went up by 138.27 points, which is 2.38%. It closed at 5958.09 points.

World Oil Prices Go Up as Market Watches Trump's Actions Against Russia

Investors are thinking about what new actions US President Donald Trump might take against Russia. They are also thinking about how these actions could affect how much oil is available. Because of this, international oil prices went up today.

In New York, the price for West Texas Intermediate crude oil for October went up by 1.7%. It closed at $63.67 per barrel.

In London, the price for Brent crude oil for November also went up by 1.7%. It closed at $67.49 per barrel.

After 20 Months, Prince Harry and King Charles III Have Tea Together

Today, Prince Harry of the UK had afternoon tea with his father, King Charles III. This was the first time they had seen each other in 20 months. It might be the first step to fix their relationship.

Harry last saw his father in February 2024. At that time, King Charles III had just said he had cancer and started treatment.

Buckingham Palace said that King Charles, who is 76 years old, had tea with his son today at Clarence House in London.

Harry, who is 40 years old, arrived in the UK on the 8th of this month. He plans to go to several events.

Harry and his American wife, Meghan, moved to California in the US in 2020. After that, Harry often spoke negatively about the British Royal Family and its rules in interviews, documentaries, and his book. He made many strong comments about his father Charles and his brother William, who will become king. Because of this, his relationship with his family completely broke.
