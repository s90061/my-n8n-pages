UN: Over 1,000 People Looking for Help Killed in Gaza Since May

The United Nations (UN) said today that more than 1,000 Palestinians have been killed by the Israeli army in Gaza. These people were trying to get food. This has happened since the "Gaza Humanitarian Fund" (GHF) started working in late May. This fund is supported by the United States and Israel.

Thameen Al-Kheetan, a spokesperson for the UN Human Rights Office, told a news agency, "As of July 21, we have recorded a total of 1,054 people killed in Gaza while trying to get food."

Al-Kheetan said that 766 of these people were killed near GHF places. Another 288 people died near aid vehicles from the UN and other groups. He clearly stated that the Israeli army killed these people.

Mohammad Abu Salmiya, the director of Al-Shifa Hospital in Gaza, told reporters today that 21 children have died from not getting enough food or from hunger in different parts of Gaza in the last three days.

United States Leaves UNESCO Again; Leader Expresses Sadness

The United States announced today that it has left the United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization (UNESCO). It said that staying in UNESCO is not good for America. Audrey Azoulay, the head of UNESCO, said she was very sad about this.

Tammy Bruce, a spokesperson for the US Department of State, said that America has left UNESCO. She said that UNESCO is unfair to Israel and promotes "ideas that cause disagreements in society and culture." So, staying in UNESCO is not good for America.

Audrey Azoulay, the head of UNESCO, said she was very sad. She stated, "I am very sad that President Donald Trump has decided again to make the US leave UNESCO." But she also said she was not surprised.

America leaving UNESCO is a problem for the organization. UNESCO was started after World War II to help countries work together in education, science, and culture to create peace. Its main office is in Paris.

During his first time as president, Trump also left other groups like the World Health Organization (WHO), the UN Human Rights Council, the Paris Agreement on climate change, and the nuclear deal with Iran.

When Joe Biden became president in 2021, he changed these decisions. He made America rejoin UNESCO, WHO, and the Paris Agreement.

Now that Trump is president again, the US is leaving these international groups once more. He has also decided to make America leave WHO. He also stopped giving money to the UN group that helps Palestinian refugees (UNRWA). These decisions are part of a review of America's involvement in UN organizations, which should finish by August.

UNESCO is most known for choosing World Heritage Sites. These include the Grand Canyon in the US and the ancient city of Palmyra in Syria.

Taipei Dangdai Art Fair Will Not Happen in 2026, Future Plans Being Reviewed

The Taipei Dangdai Art Fair's official website has announced that the event will not take place in 2026. The team will use this time to carefully rethink the fair's plans for the future. This includes looking closely at how the event will be held, when it will happen, how big it will be, and what it will look like.

The website stated that the 2026 Taipei Dangdai Art Fair will pause for one year. The fair's team will use this time to review its plans. They will check the structure, timing, size, and style of the next fair. They hope to plan its steps forward carefully, so it can keep helping the art world in Taiwan and the area.

The Taipei Dangdai Art Fair also said on its website that it wants to be a main platform for modern art in Taiwan and the whole region. "We are proud of the group we have built," the website said. This group includes art galleries, partners, people who collect art, people who choose art for shows, and all the visitors. They all help make the Taipei Dangdai Art Fair what it is.

The website thanked everyone for their help and trust. Taipei Dangdai Art Fair is honored to have worked with galleries, partners, collectors, curators, and many art lovers. "Because of everyone's involvement and help, the Taipei Dangdai Art Fair is now in its important position," the website added.

The "Taipei Dangdai Art Fair" is organized by UBS Group. It first started in Taipei in 2019. Its goal is to show the newest modern art from Asia to visitors. Art and new ideas are the two most important parts of the fair. It hopes to use art as a way for different people in society to connect and talk to each other.
