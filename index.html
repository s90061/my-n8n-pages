US Government: At Least 3 Dead in Los Angeles Police Training Center Explosion

The US Attorney General said that at least three people died in an "event" at a police training center in Los Angeles. News reports said it was an accidental explosion.

Agence France-Presse (AFP) reported that US Attorney General Pam Bondi wrote on social media platform X, "It seems there was a terrible event where at least 3 people died at a law enforcement training facility." She added that investigators are still at the site "to understand what happened."

California Governor Gavin Newsom's office said he was told about the "reported explosion." He is "watching the situation closely."

Fox News and other US media said that the event at the Biscailuz Training Center seemed to be an accident. At least three police officers died.

European Stocks Show Mixed Results After US Data Boost Fades

(Central News Agency, London, July 18, Comprehensive Foreign Report) European stock markets showed different results today. This happened after a week of gains that were helped by good economic data from the United States and positive company financial reports.

The FTSE 100 index in London went up by 19.48 points, which is 0.22%. It closed at 8992.12 points.

The DAX index in Frankfurt went down by 81.42 points, which is 0.33%. It closed at 24289.51 points.

The CAC 40 index in Paris went up slightly by 0.67 points, which is 0.01%. It closed at 7822.67 points. (Translator: Hsu Chong-che/Editor: Hsu Rui-cheng) 1140719

Typhoon Wipha Gets a Little Stronger, Heavy Rain Warning for 15 Areas

(Central News Agency, Taipei, July 18) The Central Weather Bureau gave the latest typhoon sea warning tonight. Tropical Storm Wipha has become a little stronger. Because of the typhoon's outer winds, there will be short periods of heavy rain from tonight until tomorrow. The Weather Bureau also warned 15 cities and counties about heavy rain.

The Weather Bureau said that at 11 PM tonight, Wipha's center was about 280 kilometers south of Eluanbi. It was moving northwest at 22 kilometers per hour. The average radius of its 7-level winds is 100 kilometers.

The Weather Bureau stated that Wipha's strength grew a little in the last 3 hours. Its storm circle is now entering the Bashi Channel. It will be a danger to the Bashi Channel, the southern Taiwan Strait, and the Dongsha Islands sea area. The typhoon is expected to get a little stronger, and its storm circle might become a little bigger.

The Weather Bureau issued a heavy rain warning for 15 cities and counties. Due to the typhoon's outer rainbands, there will be short periods of strong rain. From tonight until tomorrow, some mountain areas in Hualien County and Taitung County will have very heavy rain or extremely heavy rain.

Hengchun Peninsula, mountain areas in Yilan County, Nantou County, Kaohsiung City, Pingtung County, and Hualien County, Taitung County (including Orchid Island and Green Island) will have local heavy rain or very heavy rain. Keelung's north coast, Yilan, the southern areas, and mountain areas in northern Taiwan, Taichung, and Chiayi will have local heavy rain. Hualien and Taitung might have extremely heavy rain.
