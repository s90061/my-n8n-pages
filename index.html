CDMO Business Grows, Bora Pharmaceuticals' First Half EPS Reaches NT$19.5

Bora Pharmaceuticals has shared its financial report for the first half of the year. The company earned NT$9.347 billion in sales. Their CDMO business, which stands for Contract Development and Manufacturing Organization, grew well. The company's net profit for its owners was NT$2.016 billion, which is 11.9% more than last year. The earnings per share (EPS) was NT$19.5, meaning they earned almost twice their share value.

Today, Bora Pharmaceuticals announced that its CDMO business grew a lot in the first half of this year. This was because they made more products and started making new types of medicines. Their sales, including internal orders, went up by 69.5% from last year, reaching over NT$5.01 billion. However, during a time when they were combining different parts of the company, their factory in Maryland, USA, which makes sterile injections, and their factory in Zhunan, Taiwan, both had delays in making products. This slowed things down. But this problem got better in June, and they will now finish the delayed orders. The company sees clearly that they have enough orders for a long time, and the demand for their services is strong.

Bora Pharmaceuticals said they are still putting more money into their CDMO business around the world. Their factory in Canada recently added a new production line for skin medicines. This new line will help them make these medicines faster, in larger amounts, and with more flexibility. This will help the Canadian factory grow.

The Flex-Pro sterile injection production line in Maryland, USA, is planned to start working in the third quarter of this year. Also, starting from 2026, the AST isolated filling system's automatic lines will begin to operate. In addition, customers at the Maple Grove factory in Minnesota, USA, who are in the clinical trial stage, have become stronger, long-term partners with Bora Pharmaceuticals in the first half of this year. Many drug companies have started signing agreements because they want to use the benefits of making products in the USA.

Mr. Sheng Bao-hsi, the chairman of Bora Pharmaceuticals, stated that since the start of this year, the CDMO business has gained 8 new customers. Most of these new orders are handled by the Maple Grove factory in North America, which is a main center for local manufacturing. This year, the value of new orders received is US$138 million. The new orders are mostly for the Canadian factory and the sterile medicine factories. For large molecule CDMO, the San Diego factory, which was added through the merger with TaiFu Biologics, has started working. Several American drug companies that are in the later stages of developing medicines have asked about the plan to increase production by 2000 liters.

Taiwan Life Moves into Taoyuan Aerotropolis, Plans to Invest Over NT$50 Billion

The Taoyuan City Government announced on September 25 last year that Taiwan Life Insurance won the bid for a piece of land in the Taoyuan Aerotropolis Priority Industry Park, known as 'Area I'. They won with a bid of NT$13.7 billion. Today, Taiwan Life Insurance stated that the total money they plan to invest in this project is over NT$50 billion. They plan to build a multi-purpose sports center, hotels, and logistics facilities. They hope this project will become a new famous place in the area.

The Taoyuan City Government announced on September 25 last year that Taiwan Life Insurance won the bid for the land in 'Area I' of the Taoyuan Aerotropolis Priority Industry Park. They won with a bid of NT$13,749,796,800. This means they will move into the largest area within the priority industry park.

Taiwan Life Insurance released important news today to give more details. Taiwan Life and Evergreen International Storage & Transport Corporation have created a special company together. This company will invest in the land in 'Area I' of the Aerotropolis. Both sides have signed an agreement to work together and have set up this new company.

Taiwan Life explained that this project is located within the Taoyuan Aerotropolis, which is an important area in the nation's main development plan. It has good potential for growth. The piece of land for this project is very large. They hope to build a new landmark for Taoyuan, making it a key multi-purpose area.

Taiwan Life stated that they will follow the bidding rules and review comments from the Taoyuan City Government. They will build a multi-purpose sports center that meets international competition standards. They will also add shops and hotel facilities next to it to help the area grow. In addition, they will build new logistics and office buildings based on what the local market and industries need. The total investment for this project is expected to be over NT$50 billion.

Taiwan Life mentioned that the multi-purpose sports center is expected to have 8,000 seats. Besides being used for basketball, it can also be used for other types of sports events.
