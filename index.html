US and China talks finished; China says they agreed to extend the break from tariffs

Officials from the United States and China finished two days of talks today. They were trying to fix money problems and stop a trade war from getting worse. China said that both sides agreed to continue a deal they made in mid-May. This deal stopped new taxes on goods for 90 days. But China did not say when this extension would start or how long it would last.

Reuters reported that the talks did not lead to any big new agreements. However, Li Chenggang, a trade official from China, said that both countries want to extend the 90-day deal. This deal, made in mid-May, stopped new taxes on goods. But it is not clear when the extension will begin or how long it will last.

These discussions might help set up a meeting between US President Donald Trump and Chinese President Xi Jinping later this year. But President Trump said he did not ask for the meeting. Chinese officials also did not talk about it.

For many months, President Trump said he would put high taxes on goods from other countries. Now, he has made trade deals with places like the European Union and Japan. But talking with China is harder. China is a very strong economy and controls most of the world's rare earth metals. This makes the talks with the US very difficult.

In May, the US and China stopped plans to add very high taxes on each other's goods. This prevented a full stop in trade between them. But if they cannot agree, world trade and money markets could become unstable again.

To show how important these talks are, the International Monetary Fund (IMF) today said the world economy might grow more. But they also warned that if the taxes on goods go up again, it could be a big problem.

European Stocks Go Up; Investors Look at Company Money Reports

Major stock markets in Europe finished higher today. People who invest their money are now looking at company reports about how much money they made. These reports will come out this week, and investors are not focusing on trade deals anymore.

The FTSE 100 index in London went up a little, by 54.88 points. This is 0.60%. It closed at 9136.32 points.

The DAX index in Frankfurt went up more, by 247.01 points. This is 1.03%. It finished at 24217.37 points.

The CAC 40 index in Paris increased by 56.48 points. This is 0.72%. It closed at 7857.36 points.

Weather Agency Warns of Heavy Rain; People in Southern Taiwan Should Prepare

The Central Weather Agency has given a special warning for heavy rain. Because of winds from the southwest, there will be strong rain for short times. From tonight until tomorrow, some mountain areas in Pingtung County will have very heavy rain or extremely heavy rain. Also, parts of Chiayi City, Chiayi County, Tainan City, Kaohsiung City, and Pingtung County will have heavy or very heavy rain. The central part of Taiwan, Taitung, and mountain areas in the north and Yilan-Hualien could also have heavy rain.

The Central Weather Agency wants everyone to be careful of lightning and strong winds. In the mountains, please watch out for falling rocks, mudslides, and sudden floods. If you live in a low area, please be careful of flooding. Pingtung County is where the heaviest rain is expected.
