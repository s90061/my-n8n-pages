Federal Reserve Rate Cut Expectations Rise, European Stocks Close Higher

London (CNA) - Europe's main stock markets closed higher today. This happened because new information about inflation in the United States, released yesterday, showed stable prices. This made investors believe that the U.S. central bank (Federal Reserve) will lower interest rates.

The FTSE 100 index in London went up by 17.42 points, or 0.19%, ending the day at 9165.23 points.

The DAX index in Frankfurt rose by 160.81 points, or 0.67%, to reach 24185.59 points.

The CAC 40 index in Paris increased by 51.55 points, or 0.66%, closing at 7804.97 points.

Typhoon Yangliu Weakens to a Light Typhoon, Taiwan Main Island Leaves Storm Circle

Taipei (CNA) - Typhoon Yangliu is moving away. The weather agency says that Yangliu has become weaker. At 11 PM, it became a light typhoon. The main island of Taiwan is now out of the storm's danger area. The land warning is now only for Penghu and Kinmen. Especially for Kinmen, there might still be strong winds and rain until early tomorrow morning.

According to the Central Weather Administration, at 11 PM today, Typhoon Yangliu was at 23.7 degrees North latitude and 118.0 degrees East longitude. This is about 90 kilometers southwest of Kinmen. It is moving northwest at a speed of 30 to 33 kilometers per hour.

The weather agency said that Yangliu has become weaker in the last 3 hours. Its center is now near the sea southwest of Kinmen. It will continue to move northwest. The main island of Taiwan is now out of its storm circle. But it still threatens Penghu and Kinmen, where winds and rain are continuing. The typhoon is expected to get weaker and its storm circle will get smaller.

Forecaster Chu Mei-lin said in a press conference that Yangliu is moving northwest quickly. It is expected to land in China late tonight. It will also get weaker. Taiwan's main island is now out of the storm's circle. The land warning is only for Penghu and Kinmen. Kinmen should especially watch out for strong winds and rain from tonight until early tomorrow morning.

Chu Mei-lin also said that the wind in eastern Taiwan is from the south-southeast. So, Hualien and Taitung will still have a lot of rain until tomorrow morning. The cloud systems on the east and south sides of Yangliu are strong. So, the southwest coast and Penghu will still have rain on and off.

Chu Mei-lin noted that after tomorrow morning, the rain will become less, and the weather will get better. Most places will be cloudy to sunny. Western Taiwan should watch out for thunderstorms in the afternoon, especially in the central mountains where there might be heavy rain. Eastern Taiwan will have rain on and off.

Chu Mei-lin said that the weather will be quite stable until August 17th. Starting from the evening of August 16th, there will be more rain due to moisture from the south. On August 19th and 20th, the weather will return to being cloudy to sunny.

According to the weather agency's observations, from 12 AM on August 12th to 11 PM on August 13th, the areas with the most rain were: Dahanshan in Pingtung County with 453.5 millimeters, Fengnan in Hualien County with 381.5 millimeters, Chishang in Taitung County with 351.5 millimeters, and Nantianchi in Kaohsiung City with 250.5 millimeters. The areas with strong winds were: Orchid Island and Green Island with winds above level 17, Dongjiyu Island with level 13, Taitung with level 12, Chenggong and New Taipei with level 11, Xinwu, Su-ao, Pengjiayu, Taipei, and Sun Moon Lake with level 10.
