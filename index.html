French, UK, German Leaders to Discuss Peace in Ukraine

The French president's office announced that leaders from France, the UK, and Germany will hold an online meeting tomorrow. This meeting will include countries that support Kyiv (Ukraine). They will talk about how to make peace in Ukraine. They call these countries the "Coalition of the Willing."

The French president's office also said why this meeting is happening. French President Emmanuel Macron, UK Prime Minister Keir Starmer, and German Chancellor Friedrich Merz will lead it. It's because the leaders of the US and Russia met yesterday. Also, Ukraine's President Volodymyr Zelenskyy will go to Washington on the 18th to talk with US President Donald Trump.

After his talks with Russian President Vladimir Putin yesterday, Trump said at a press conference that they agreed on many things. But they did not reach a full agreement.

Later, Trump spoke to Fox News. He added that he and Putin agreed on how the Russia-Ukraine war could end. It would involve exchanging land and the US giving some kind of safety promise.
