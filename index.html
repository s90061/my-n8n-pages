Trump to Meet Putin; Officials from US, UK, Europe, and Ukraine to Hold Talks First

London, August 9th (CNA) - A spokesperson from the British Prime Minister's office said that British Foreign Secretary David Lammy and US Vice President JD Vance will meet in the UK today. They will meet with allies from Ukraine and Europe. They will talk about how US President Donald Trump wants to bring peace to Ukraine.

Reuters reported that the Downing Street spokesperson also said that before this security meeting, British Prime Minister Keir Starmer spoke with Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelenskyy. They talked about Trump's ideas to end the war between Russia and Ukraine.

Trump announced yesterday that he will meet with Russian President Vladimir Putin on August 15th in Alaska, USA.

The British Prime Minister's office spokesperson said: "The Prime Minister spoke with President Zelenskyy this morning. They are looking forward to the meeting today with national security advisors from Europe, Ukraine, and the United States. The British Foreign Secretary and the US Vice President will lead this meeting."

The spokesperson also said that both Starmer and Zelenskyy "agreed that this meeting is very important to discuss how to achieve a fair and lasting peace."

Polish President Nawrocki to Visit US in Early September, Will Meet Trump

Warsaw, August 9th (CNA) - An official from the office of Poland's new President Karol Nawrocki said today that US President Donald Trump has invited Nawrocki to Washington in early September.

Reuters reported that Nawrocki became the President of Poland on August 6th. He has often said that having a good relationship between Poland and the United States is very important.

During his election campaign, Nawrocki received support from Poland's main nationalist opposition party, the "Law and Justice Party." He also met with Trump in the Oval Office before the Polish election in May, and Trump supported his run for president.

Pawel Szefernaker, an official from the Polish President's office, wrote on X (a social media platform): "In an official letter on the day he became president, US President Trump invited Polish President Nawrocki to the White House for a formal working meeting on September 3, 2025."
