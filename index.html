Weather Agency Warns of Heavy Rain; Chiayi, Tainan, Kaohsiung, Pingtung Should Prepare for Strong Rains

The Central Weather Agency has issued a heavy rain warning. Because of the southwest air current, there will be short periods of very strong rain. Today, some mountain areas in Kaohsiung City and Pingtung County will have heavy or very heavy rain. Chiayi City, Chiayi County, Tainan City, Kaohsiung City, and Pingtung County will have some heavy rain. Central Taiwan and Taitung might also have some heavy rain.

The Central Weather Agency reminds people to be careful of lightning and strong winds. In mountain areas, be careful of landslides, falling rocks, mudslides, and flash floods. In low-lying areas, please be careful of flooding. The areas expecting very heavy rain are Kaohsiung City and Pingtung County.

Trump has not yet sent a tariff letter to India, like he did to more than ten other countries. On April 2, Trump announced he would raise tariffs on Indian goods sold in the US to 26%. But he soon stopped these "equal" tariffs.
