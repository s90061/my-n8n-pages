Heavy Rain Expected in Hengchun Peninsula and Taitung Due to Strong Thunderstorms

Taiwan's weather office has issued a special report for heavy rain. Strong thunderstorm clouds are forming, which means there will be heavy rain for short periods. Today, there might be heavy rain or very heavy rain in some parts of the Hengchun Peninsula. Also, there is a chance of heavy rain in Taitung.

The weather office also warned people to watch out for lightning and strong winds. In the mountains, people should be careful of landslides, falling rocks, and rivers overflowing. In low-lying areas, people should be careful of floods.

Trump Says US Will Support Ukraine's Safety, Details Being Planned

Today at the White House, US President Trump said that he has not yet talked about the exact details of how the US will keep Ukraine safe. But he said again that the US will continue to help Ukraine.

Reuters reported that finding ways to make Ukraine safe is a big problem in ending Russia's war against Ukraine.

Trump also said that on August 15, he met with Russian President Vladimir Putin in Alaska, USA. During their meeting, they talked about reducing the size of the US and Russia's large nuclear weapons after the problem in Ukraine is solved.

Trump told reporters in the Oval Office at the White House, "We want to remove nuclear weapons... but we must end this war."

When reporters asked why Putin did not want to meet with Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelenskyy, Trump answered, "Because he does not like Zelenskyy."

Tseng Chun-hsin Loses in US Open First Round, Still Waiting for First Grand Slam Win

After three years, Taiwan's top tennis player, Tseng Chun-hsin, played in the main competition of the US Open again today. Sadly, he lost to Belgium's good player Bergs with scores of 3-6, 4-6, 3-6. This means he will have to wait longer for his first win in a major tennis tournament.

The US Open is the last of the four biggest tennis tournaments. Tseng Chun-hsin, who is ranked 118th in the world, got a spot directly in the men's singles main draw this time. He was also Taiwan's only hope for singles. In the first round, he played against Zizou Bergs from Belgium, who is ranked 48th. Tseng wanted to get his first win in a Grand Slam main competition.

Tseng started the match wanting to challenge his opponent. In the first set, he had a close fight with Bergs. But in the eighth game, he lost his serve and then lost the first set 3-6. Luckily, Tseng adjusted well in the second set. He used his opponent's unstable serves to get ahead 4-2. But then, he also had problems with his serve. He lost his serve twice and then lost the second set 4-6.

With no other choice, Tseng played the third set trying his best. But he could not find his good form again. He lost his serve twice and fell far behind at 1-5. Even though he broke his opponent's serve once to try and stay in the game, he hit the ball into the net when trying a volley at the end. He lost the match 3-6. This means he will not go to the second round of the US Open men's singles. He is still waiting for his first win in a Grand Slam main competition.
