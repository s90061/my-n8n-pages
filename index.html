Nvidia and OpenAI Work Together to Build Super AI Center

The chip company Nvidia and the artificial intelligence company OpenAI have announced they will work together. Nvidia will build a new AI data center for OpenAI. This data center will use 10 gigawatts of electricity and have millions of powerful computer chips (GPUs) to help develop advanced AI models.

Nvidia will invest up to 100 billion US dollars (about 3 trillion New Taiwan Dollars) over time. The first large system will be ready in the second half of 2026. Nvidia's CEO, Jensen Huang, said that Nvidia and OpenAI have worked well together for 10 years. This new investment will help create a 10-gigawatt system to start a new age of AI.

OpenAI's CEO, Sam Altman, said that computing power is very important. He believes it will be the base for future economies. He added that the system built with Nvidia will lead to new AI discoveries. It will also help people and companies grow at the same level.

The Wall Street Journal reported that Nvidia and OpenAI, two big companies in the AI race, are now working as partners. OpenAI will be able to build and use Nvidia systems that use at least 10 gigawatts of power. They will use these to create their own AI data centers. These centers will train and run new AI models. The electricity used will be equal to the power used by 8 million homes.

This huge investment in running AI models is meant to create "superintelligence." This means AI that is smarter, faster, and stronger than humans.

This partnership shows a strong belief in making AI models better and better. It is a belief that hundreds of billions of dollars from investors, companies, governments, and banks will create AI models that can greatly change how the economy and society work today. It shows a future economy that will need much more computing power than we have now.

Porsche Lowers Expectations, European Stock Markets Fall

Porsche, a well-known sports car company, has said that its financial predictions for the future are lower. This made the company's stock price, and also the stock price of its main company, Volkswagen, drop a lot. Because of this, and because investors are still looking for new signs of economic growth, most major European stock markets closed lower today.

The FTSE 100 index in London went up a little, by 10.01 points, or 0.11%. It closed at 9226.68 points.

The DAX index in Frankfurt went down by 112.36 points, or 0.48%. It closed at 23527.05 points.

The CAC 40 index in Paris went down by 23.48 points, or 0.30%. It closed at 7830.11 points.

Typhoon Kajasa Stops More Train Services on September 23

Because of Typhoon Kajasa, Taiwan Railways (TRA) announced changes to its train services. TRA had first said that the South-Link Line and Taitung Line would stop running before noon on September 23. But late at night, TRA changed its information. They added that all trains on the North-Link Line would also stop running before 12 PM on September 23.

The Central Weather Administration is still telling people about the typhoon warnings for both land and sea. TRA updated its train schedule late at night. Based on the latest weather information, all trains on the North-Link Line will stop before 12 PM on September 23. Except for trains 415 and 408, which will stop completely, other trains will only go as far as Yilan, Luodong, or Su'ao New Station.

For the South-Link Line, many trains from Xinzuoying to Taitung will not run. These include trains 3001, 411, 3305, 3003, 3051, 301, 415, 3053, 3057, 423, and 3061. Trains 3005, 371, and 161 will run only to Chaozhou.

Train 727 from Chaozhou to Taitung will not run. From Taitung to Xinzuoying, trains 162 (Chaozhou to Nangang will run as normal), 3006, 304, 306, 3018 will not run. Train 372 (Chaozhou to Taichung will run as normal) will not run. The Blue Skin trains 5898 and 5899 between Taitung and Fangliao will also not run.

The Taitung Line will also stop service before 12 PM tomorrow. Many trains from Taitung to Hualien will not run. These include 401 (Hualien to Shulin will run as normal), 4503, 4507, 407 (Hualien to Shulin will run as normal), 4517, 4519, 411 (Hualien to Shulin will run as normal), 301, 415 (Hualien to Shulin will run as normal), 4521, 417 (Hualien to Shulin will run as normal), 653 (Hualien to Changhua will run as normal), 419 (Hualien to Shulin will run as normal), and 4513 (Hualien to Heping will run as normal), and 4015.

From Hualien to Taitung, trains 4504, 4508, 4512, 4514, 306, and 602 will not run. Trains 4516, 402, 406, 408, 410, and 472 will only go as far as Hualien. Train 207 will not run between Shoufeng and Hualien, but will run normally between Hualien and Shulin.
