Heavy Rain Stops Alishan Forest Railway on July 28th

The Chiayi County government announced today that work and school are stopped because of heavy rain. The Alishan Forest Railway and Cultural Heritage Office said that the train service is stopped on all lines as a safety measure. They will announce when the trains will start running again later.

If you have bought tickets, you can get a full refund within one year from your travel date. You can do this at any Alishan Forest Railway station or at any 7-Eleven IBON machine in Taiwan. You can find more information on the Alishan Forest Railway website.

Shuilin Township in Yunlin Stops Work and School on July 28 Due to Heavy Rain

Because of heavy rain from a low-pressure system, many low areas in Shuilin Township, Yunlin County, are flooded. The Shuilin Township Office announced at 8 AM today that work and school are canceled for the whole day.

Chiayi County Has Very Heavy Rain; Taibao and Other Areas Have High Flood Warnings

Because of a low-pressure area and southwest winds, Chiayi County had heavy or very heavy rain today in some places. The Chiayi County government said that Taibao City, Puzi City, Xingang Township, and other areas are now under the highest level flood warning. They have already closed the east and west lanes of the Zhonghe Interchange on Provincial Highway 82 as a safety measure.

The Central Weather Administration announced a heavy rain warning. It said that the low-pressure area and southwest winds will bring strong, short rains. Today, Chiayi City and Chiayi County will have heavy or very heavy rain in some areas. In Taibao, Chiayi County, the rain reached 118 millimeters per hour early in the morning. Xingang Township, Minxiong Township, and Shuishang Township also had more than 90 millimeters of rain per hour.

A resident named Cai from Puzi, who lives near Chang Gung Memorial Hospital in Chiayi, said it rained with thunder almost all night. When he woke up in the morning, his garage was flooded, which surprised him. He has lived in that area for many years and this is the first time this has happened.

The Chiayi County government also announced that Provincial Highway 82 and County Road 175 interchanges in Shuishang Township are flooded. Police went to the scene and found the water was knee-deep. They have closed the east and west lanes of the Zhonghe Interchange on Provincial Highway 82 and the north and south lanes of County Road 175 as a safety measure. Areas with the highest flood warning include Taibao City, Puzi City, Dalin Township, Minxiong Township, Xingang Township, Liujiao Township, and Dongshi Township.

The county government said that the service road next to Provincial Highway 82 at the 13.1-kilometer mark in Puzi City was also flooded. The Puzi Police Station's Daxing Joint Office found that the water was too deep for safe travel and closed the road as a safety measure.
