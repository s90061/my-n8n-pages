European Stocks Go Up a Little as Market Waits for US Interest Rate News

(London, July 30, Central News Agency) Investors are waiting for financial reports from big US tech companies. They are also waiting for the US Federal Reserve (Fed) to share its decision about interest rates. Because of this, the US dollar has gone up from its low point recently. The main stock markets in Europe closed a little higher today.

The FTSE 100 index in London went up a small amount, 0.62 points, or 0.01%. It closed at 9136.94 points.

The DAX index in Frankfurt went up a little, 44.85 points, or 0.19%. It closed at 24262.22 points.

The CAC 40 index in Paris went up slightly, 4.60 points, or 0.06%. It closed at 7861.96 points. (Translator: Chen Cheng-chien / Editor: Chang Cheng-chien) July 31, 2025

Heavy Rain Expected in Mountains of Nantou, Yunlin, Chiayi, Kaohsiung, Pingtung Due to Southwest Airflow

(Taipei, July 31, Central News Agency) The Central Weather Administration has given a heavy rain warning. A southwest airflow is causing short periods of very strong rain. Today, the mountain areas of Nantou County, Yunlin County, Chiayi County, Kaohsiung City, and Pingtung County will have very heavy or extremely heavy local rain.

Also, Nantou County and Tainan City mountain areas will have local heavy or very heavy rain. Taichung City, Changhua County, Chiayi City, Chiayi County, Tainan City, Kaohsiung City, Pingtung County, and Taitung County will have local heavy rain or very heavy rain. Yunlin and Miaoli areas might have local heavy rain.

Please be careful of lightning and strong winds. In mountain areas, please be careful of landslides, falling rocks, mudslides, and sudden floods. In low areas, please be careful of flooding. Areas with extremely heavy rain are: Nantou County, Yunlin County, Chiayi County, Kaohsiung City, Pingtung County. Areas with very heavy rain are: Tainan City. Chiayi, Kaohsiung, Pingtung Mountains Must Prepare for Very Heavy Rain; Nantou, Yunlin, Tainan Should Watch for Heavy Rain

(Taipei, July 30, Central News Agency) The Central Weather Administration has issued a heavy rain warning. A southwest airflow is causing short periods of strong rain. From tonight until tomorrow, the mountain areas of Chiayi County, Kaohsiung City, and Pingtung County will have local very heavy or extremely heavy rain. Nantou County and the mountain areas of Yunlin County and Tainan City will have local heavy or very heavy rain.

In addition, Taichung City, Changhua County, Chiayi City, Chiayi County, Tainan City, Kaohsiung City, Pingtung County, and Taitung County will have local heavy rain or very heavy rain. Yunlin area and Miaoli mountain area might have local heavy rain.

The Weather Administration warns people to be careful of lightning and strong winds. In mountain areas, be very careful of landslides, falling rocks, mudslides, and sudden floods. In low-lying areas, be careful of flooding.

Areas with extremely heavy rain are: Chiayi County, Kaohsiung City, Pingtung County. Areas with very heavy rain are: Nantou County, Yunlin County, Tainan City. (Editor: Yang Chao-yen) July 30, 2025
