European Stocks Go Up; People Look at Company Reports

Today, the main stock markets in Europe closed higher. Investors are now looking at company reports that will come out this week, instead of trade agreements.

The FTSE 100 index in London went up a little, by 54.88 points or 0.60%. It closed at 9136.32 points.

The DAX index in Frankfurt went up by 247.01 points or 1.03%. It closed at 24217.37 points.

The CAC 40 index in Paris went up by 56.48 points or 0.72%. It closed at 7857.36 points.

Banqiao Butcher Cuts Person with Knife 7 Times; Police Arrest Him, Injured Person is Safe in Hospital

Tonight, there was a knife attack in Banqiao, New Taipei. A man named Lin went to collect money that someone owed him. They argued, and the butcher, who sells meat, used a pig-killing knife to cut the person 7 times. The police caught him. The injured person was taken to the hospital and is now safe. The police said they will send the butcher to court for causing harm on July 30th.

The Haishan Police Station said late tonight that they got a call around 8 PM. They went to Jiangning Market on Shuangshi Road Section 3 to deal with the problem. Someone was hurt and bleeding there.

The police found that two men had a fight because of money problems. The butcher, Mr. Lin, owed money. He was angry because the other man kept asking for the money. So, tonight, he used a knife from his meat stall to cut the person's back and arm. The person had 7 knife wounds.

Many people saw it and were very scared. They ran away. The police said it was good that the injured person was taken to Far Eastern Memorial Hospital for treatment. He was awake and not in danger.

The police caught Mr. Lin at the place where it happened. They took him back to the police station to ask about the money problems. They will send him to the New Taipei District Prosecutor's Office on July 30th for investigation, based on the charge of causing harm.

Weather Agency Warns of Heavy Rain: Chiayi, Tainan, Kaohsiung, Pingtung Need to Prepare for Heavy Rains

The Central Weather Agency in Taipei announced a heavy rain warning today, July 29. A southwest air current is bringing strong, short-term rain. From tonight until tomorrow, some mountain areas in Pingtung County will have very heavy rain or extremely heavy rain. Chiayi City, Chiayi County, Tainan City, Kaohsiung City, and Pingtung County will have heavy rain or very heavy rain in some areas. Central Taiwan, Taitung area, and mountain areas in Northern Taiwan and Yilan-Hualien might also have heavy rain.

The Central Weather Agency reminds everyone to be careful of lightning and strong winds. In mountain areas, please be very careful about landslides, falling rocks, mudslides, and sudden floods from rivers. In low areas, please be careful of flooding. The area with extremely heavy rain is Pingtung County.
