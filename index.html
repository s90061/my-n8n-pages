Singapore Critical Infrastructure Hit by Cyberattack, Experts Suspect China Link

Singapore announced yesterday that its key computer systems were attacked. Security experts believe the attackers are a spy group connected to China. Authorities are dealing with the situation.

K. Shanmugam, who is Singapore's Minister for National Security and Home Affairs, said in a speech: "UNC3886 is a serious danger to us. It could harm our country's safety. It targets important places that provide necessary services and valuable strategic goals."

Shanmugam did not give details about the attack or what happened because of security risks.

Mandiant, a security company owned by Google, said that UNC3886 is "a spy group linked to China." This group has attacked defense, technology, and phone companies in the US and Asia before.

China always says it does not do cyber spying.

Reuters reported this week that Taiwan's computer chip industry and investment experts have been targets of many cyber spy attacks. These attacks are believed to be from hackers connected to China.

Researchers from Proofpoint, a cybersecurity company, said that at least three different groups linked to China did these attacks. They happened mostly from March to June this year, and some might still be happening.

Weather Agency Issues Heavy Rain Warning for Hualien and Taitung Mountains

The Central Weather Administration has given a heavy rain warning. Because of the edges of a typhoon, there will be short periods of very strong rain. Today, there will be heavy or very heavy rain in the mountains of Hualien County and Taitung County. The Hengchun Peninsula, mountains in Yilan County, Nantou County, Kaohsiung City, Pingtung County, and Hualien County, Taitung County (including Orchid Island and Green Island) will have local heavy rain or very heavy rain. There is a chance of local heavy rain in Keelung's north coast, Yilan, southern areas, and mountains in northern Taiwan, Taichung, and Chiayi. Please be careful of lightning, strong winds, and rivers rising quickly. In mountain areas, please watch out for landslides, falling rocks, and mudslides. In low areas, please be careful of water collecting. The areas with very heavy rain are Hualien County and Taitung County.

US Government: At Least 3 Dead in Los Angeles Police Training Center Explosion

The US Attorney General stated that at least 3 people died in an "incident" at a police training center in Los Angeles. News reports said it was an accidental explosion.

AFP reported that US Attorney General Pam Bondi wrote on X (social media) that "it seems there was a terrible incident at a law enforcement training place, causing at least 3 deaths." Investigators are at the scene "still figuring out what happened."

California Governor Gavin Newsom's office said he was told about the "reported explosion" and is "watching the situation closely."

Fox News and other US media reported that the event at the Biscailuz Training Center seemed to be an accident. It caused at least 3 police officers to die.

The "LA Times" said, using unnamed sources, that the explosion happened when the explosive team at the training center was moving explosives.
