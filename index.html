European Stocks Rise, People Look at Company Reports

Today, the main stock markets in Europe went up. People who invest are now looking at company financial reports that will come out this week, instead of looking at trade agreements.

The FTSE 100 index in London went up a little, by 54.88 points or 0.60%, and closed at 9136.32 points.

The DAX index in Frankfurt went up by 247.01 points or 1.03%, and closed at 24217.37 points.

The CAC 40 index in Paris went up by 56.48 points or 0.72%, and closed at 7857.36 points.

Butcher in Banqiao Cuts Person 7 Times with Knife, Police Arrest Him, Injured Person is Safe in Hospital

In Banqiao, New Taipei, there was a knife attack tonight. A man named Lin came to collect money owed to him. They argued, and the butcher used a knife from his stall to cut the man 7 times. The police arrested the butcher. The injured man was taken to the hospital and is now safe. The police said they will send the butcher to court on July 30th for causing harm.

The Haishan Police Station said late tonight that they got a call around 8 PM to go to Jiangning Market on Shuangshi Road Section 3 because of a fight. Someone was hurt and bleeding there.

The police found that two men had a physical fight because of money problems. The butcher, Mr. Lin, owed money. He seemed upset that the other person kept asking for the money back. So, tonight, he used a kitchen knife from his meat stall to cut the man on his back and arm, causing 7 cuts.

Many people saw what happened and were very scared, so they ran away. The police said that luckily, the man who was cut was taken to Far Eastern Memorial Hospital. He is awake and not in danger.

The police caught the butcher right there. They took him to the police station to ask about the money problems. They will send him to the New Taipei District Prosecutor's Office on July 30th for investigation because of the harm he caused.

Weather Bureau Warns of Heavy Rain in Southern Taiwan

Taiwan's Central Weather Bureau has given a special warning for heavy rain. Due to strong winds from the southwest, there will be short periods of heavy rain. From tonight until tomorrow, some mountain areas in Pingtung County will have very heavy rain. Also, parts of Chiayi City, Chiayi County, Tainan City, Kaohsiung City, and Pingtung County will have heavy rain. Some areas in central Taiwan, Taitung, and mountain areas in northern Taiwan and Yilan/Hualien might also have heavy rain.

The Central Weather Bureau reminds people to be careful of lightning and strong winds. In mountain areas, please be careful of landslides, falling rocks, mudslides, and sudden floods. In low areas, please be careful of floods. Pingtung County is expected to have very heavy rain.
