Oracle's Stock Prices Go Up a Lot as People Feel Good About AI; US Stock Market Ends Higher, S&P Reaches New High

People are hopeful about artificial intelligence (AI). Because of this, the stock price of Oracle, a big American software company, went up a lot. Also, people think the US central bank (Fed) will lower interest rates soon. So, most main US stock market indexes ended higher today, and the S&P 500 index reached its highest point ever.

The Dow Jones Industrial Average went down by 220.42 points, or 0.48%. It closed at 45490.92 points.

The S&P 500 index went up a little, by 19.43 points, or 0.30%. It closed at 6532.04 points.

The Nasdaq index went up by 6.57 points, or 0.03%. It closed at 21886.06 points.

The Philadelphia Semiconductor Index went up by 138.27 points, or 2.38%. It closed at 5958.09 points.

International Oil Prices Increase as People Wait to See What Trump Will Do About Russia

Today, international oil prices went up. Investors are thinking about whether former US President Donald Trump will punish Russia more. They are also thinking about how this might affect the supply of oil.

The price for West Texas Intermediate crude oil for October delivery, traded in New York, went up by 1.7%. It closed at $63.67 per barrel.

The price for Brent crude oil for November delivery, traded in London, also went up by 1.7%. It closed at $67.49 per barrel.

After 20 Months, Prince Harry of UK Drinks Tea with His Father, King Charles III

Today, Prince Harry of the UK had afternoon tea with his father, King Charles III. This was the first time the father and son met in 20 months. It might be the first step to fix their problems.

Harry last saw his father in February 2024. At that time, King Charles III had just announced he had cancer and started treatment.

Buckingham Palace said that Charles, who is 76 years old, had afternoon tea with his son today at Clarence House in London.

Harry, who is 40 years old, arrived in the UK on September 8th. He plans to attend several events.

Harry and his American wife, Meghan, moved to live in California, USA, in 2020. After that, Harry often spoke badly about the British Royal Family and its rules in interviews, documentaries, and his own book. Many of his strong comments were about his father Charles and his brother, Prince William, who is next in line to the throne. Because of this, his relationship with his family completely broke.
