Strong Convective Cloud Systems, Hengchun Peninsula Should Beware of Heavy Rain, Taitung County Should Note Heavy Rain

The Central Weather Bureau has given a heavy rain warning. Strong storm clouds are forming, and there might be heavy rain for a short time. Today, the Hengchun Peninsula could have local heavy rain or very heavy rain. Taitung County might also have local heavy rain.

The Central Weather Bureau also said to be careful of lightning and strong winds. In the mountains, please watch out for landslides, falling rocks, and rising rivers. In low areas, be careful of flooding.

Trump Says US Will Support Ukraine's Security, Specific Details Being Worked Out

US President Trump said at the White House today that he has not yet talked about the exact plans for Ukraine's security. But he repeated that the US will support Ukraine.

Reuters reported that possible security for Ukraine is a big problem to solve the war where Russia attacked Ukraine.

Trump also said that on August 15, he met with Russian President Vladimir Putin in Alaska, USA. They talked about reducing the size of the large nuclear weapons of both the US and Russia after the Ukraine problem is solved.

Trump told reporters in the White House Oval Office, "We want to remove nuclear weapons... but we must end this war."

When reporters asked why Putin did not want to meet with Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelenskyy, Trump answered, "Because he does not like Zelenskyy."

Tseng Chun-hsin Loses in US Open Men's Singles First Round, Still Waiting for First Grand Slam Win

After three years, Tseng Chun-hsin, "Taiwan's No. 1," played in the US Open again. Today, he played in the first round of men's singles. Sadly, he lost to Belgium's good player Bergs with scores of 3-6, 4-6, 3-6. He will have to wait longer for his first win in a Grand Slam main draw.

The US Open is the last of the four big tennis tournaments. Tseng Chun-hsin, ranked 118th in the world, got a direct spot in the men's singles main draw this time. He is the only hope for Taiwan in singles. In the first round, he played against Zizou Bergs from Belgium, who is ranked 48th in the world. Tseng wanted to get his first win in a Grand Slam main draw.

Tseng went into the match wanting to attack his opponent. In the first set, Tseng fought hard with Bergs. But in the 8th game, Tseng's serve was broken, and he lost the set 3-6. Luckily, Tseng adjusted well in the second set. He used the chance when Bergs' serves were not good and was leading 4-2. But then, Tseng also had problems with his serve. His serve was broken twice, and he lost the set 4-6.

Tseng had no choice but to fight hard in the third set. But he still could not play well. He lost his serve twice and was far behind at 1-5. Even though he broke Bergs' serve back and tried to keep the match going, he hit the net when trying to volley. So, he lost the match 3-6. He cannot go to the second round of the US Open men's singles, and he is still waiting for his first Grand Slam main draw win.
