Countries Send Comfort After Landslide Lake Causes Damage in Hualien

A landslide lake overflowed at Mata'an Creek in Hualien, causing damage. The Hualien rescue team has reported 16 people dead and 14 missing. The offices of Canada, the UK, Australia, Belgium, and Israel in Taiwan have sent messages of comfort on social media. They want Hualien people to know they are thinking of them.

Following messages from Sandra Oudkirk, Deputy Director of the American Institute in Taiwan (AIT), the Japan-Taiwan Exchange Association, and the Czech Economic and Cultural Office, the Canadian Trade Office in Taipei shared its sadness on Facebook today. They sent their deepest condolences to families who lost loved ones and everyone else affected.

The Canadian Trade Office in Taipei also hoped that those who died can rest in peace, and those who survived will find strength and support. Canada stands with the people of Hualien during this difficult time. They also sincerely thanked the rescue workers, emergency teams, and volunteers, calling them a warm light of hope in the disaster.

The British Office in Taipei said on Facebook that Ruth Bradley-Jones, the British Representative in Taiwan, is still watching the disaster in Hualien. She feels sad for all the people affected and their livelihoods, especially families who lost close relatives. She also sent her respects to the local and central government teams and social groups who are working hard to help.

The Australian Office on Facebook sent its sincere condolences to all Taiwanese people affected by the typhoon. Many families lost loved ones, have injured family members, or still have missing family members. Their hearts are with these families.

The Belgian Office in Taipei wrote on Facebook that it is deeply saddened by the severe damage in Guangfu Township, Hualien. They sent their deepest condolences to the families of those who died. They are also thinking of those who are missing and those who had to leave their homes. They hope the rescue teams find strength.

The Israel Economic and Cultural Office in Taipei also said on Facebook that they sincerely offer comfort to all affected people. They hope everyone's lives can return to normal soon and asked everyone to stay safe.

Lu Shiow-yen Joins Lo Chih-chiang for Meal, Offers Support

Taichung Mayor Lu Shiow-yen joined KMT party leader candidate Lo Chih-chiang tonight at a famous "Taichung Rouyuan" shop for a live broadcast. Lu Shiow-yen said that this shop has been open for almost 100 years and is one of her favorite places to eat. She hopes to help the economy using the candidate's popularity. She also sent her good wishes and support to Lo Chih-chiang.

Last week, Lu Shiow-yen ate sticky rice cakes with another party leader candidate, Hau Lung-pin. Tonight, she joined Lo Chih-chiang, a legislator who is running for KMT party leader, to eat rouyuan. Lu Shiow-yen said that because all the party leader candidates are very popular, her job as mayor is to help the economy. So, she is using the candidates' popularity to help Taichung's economy.

Lu Shiow-yen explained why she chose "Taichung Rouyuan." She said this shop in Taichung is almost 100 years old and is one of her favorite places to eat. She has been eating there for decades. When she was a news director for a TV station, her apartment was across the street from "Taichung Rouyuan." So, she often went there to enjoy rouyuan. A shop that only sells rouyuan and has been open for almost 100 years, even getting a Michelin Bib Gourmand award, is truly a pride for Taichung.

About Lo Chih-chiang running for party leader, Lu Shiow-yen said she will give him support and good wishes. She believes elections are happy events. She feels it's her duty as a party member to give opportunities to other members who are willing to take on responsibilities and serve. She must vote for good leaders. She will support and wish good luck to every candidate.

Lo Chih-chiang said that many talented people are running in this party leader election, and everyone is great. He believes that whoever wins the KMT party leader position will be good for the party. He himself tries hard but stays calm during the process. Everyone is competing fairly. If they get the party members' good wishes, they will naturally win. Winning is the start of taking on responsibility. If they don't win, they should fully support the party leader. He thinks this is the right attitude. He said Lu Shiow-yen is someone everyone hopes to get good wishes from, so he visited her today.

Lo Chih-chiang, who was born in Hualien, thanked Lu Shiow-yen for starting a "return-to-home rebuilding allowance" and sending Taichung City Fire Department workers to help in the disaster area of Guangfu Township, Hualien. He said that he thanked Lu Shiow-yen on behalf of Hualien legislator Fu Kun-chi and indigenous legislator Kao Chin Su-mei. He will also suggest that the central government consider starting a similar "return-to-home rebuilding allowance" like Taichung City Government.
