Chairman Xin Jie Missing for 24 Days, Vanke Says He Resigned for Personal Reasons

Xin Jie, the chairman of a large Chinese property company called Vanke Group, was reportedly taken away for investigation in September. He was missing for 24 days, which caused much talk. Today, Vanke Company announced that Mr. Xin Jie has given his resignation because of personal reasons. The company board has chosen Huang Liping to be the new chairman.

According to Pengpai News, Vanke Company announced today that its board of directors received Mr. Xin Jie's written resignation on October 12. Mr. Xin Jie asked to leave his roles as a non-executive director and chairman of Vanke Company due to personal reasons. After leaving, he will not hold any positions at Vanke Company.

Besides being the chairman of Vanke Group, Xin Jie was also the chairman and Party Secretary of Shenzhen Metro Group. Shenzhen Metro Group is currently the biggest shareholder of Vanke Group. Mr. Xin Jie's speech as the Party Secretary and chairman of Shenzhen Metro Group is still on the "Leaders' Speeches" part of Shenzhen Metro Group's official website.

Many Chinese finance social media accounts and online messages said that Xin Jie was taken away on September 18 while attending a meeting in Shenzhen. But it is not clear if the people who took Xin Jie were police officers from the local public security department or staff from the discipline inspection department.

Pengpai News reported that Xin Jie was last seen in public at the end of August this year. At that time, Kone Elevator, Shenzhen Metro Group, and Vanke Group signed an agreement in Shenzhen, and Xin Jie was there as an important guest.

The report also said that the 24th meeting of Vanke Company's 20th board of directors chose Huang Liping as the chairman. His term will start from the date the board approves it and last until the end of the 20th board's term.

The announcement shows that Huang Liping is a director and a member of the audit committee of Vanke Company. He is also the Deputy Party Secretary, director, and general manager of Shenzhen Metro Group Co., Ltd.

The Taiwan stock market dropped by 800 points at the start of trading. The New Taiwan Dollar also fell, going below 30.6 against the US Dollar.

China put controls on rare earth exports, and US President Trump criticized Beijing for its unfriendly trade actions. He announced a new 100% tax on Chinese imports starting in November, which made the US stock market fall sharply on October 10.

During the holiday, US stocks fell heavily. This caused many people to sell their shares. Today, the Taiwan stock market opened with a big drop of over 800 points. At the same time, the New Taiwan Dollar also fell. It opened at 30.57 this morning and quickly went below 30.6. It reached a low of 30.62, losing 7.5 cents. But the fall did not get worse.

Bank experts said that people were worried about the US-China trade war getting more serious. This bad news could cause both the stock market and the currency in Taiwan to fall a lot. However, President Trump posted a message early in the morning saying that the US wants to help China, not harm it. His words seemed softer and helped calm the market's sad feelings. The financial market might correct itself, but it probably won't be too scared.
