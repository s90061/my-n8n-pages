Weather Agency Issues Heavy Rain Warning for Pingtung County

The Central Weather Agency has sent out a special warning for heavy rain. A low-pressure area is bringing short, strong rains. Today, Pingtung County may have heavy or very heavy rain in some areas. Tainan, Kaohsiung, Matsu, and mountain areas in Chiayi might also have heavy rain. Please be careful of lightning and strong winds. Also, watch out for water building up in low areas.

Thailand Agrees to Stop Fighting with Cambodia, Hopes for Talks Soon

Thailand's government announced today that it agrees to stop fighting with Cambodia. They also want to start talks to end the strong conflict that has lasted for several days. At least 33 people have died in these fights.

Agence France-Presse reported that before this, U.S. President Trump said he had spoken to the leaders of both Cambodia and Thailand. They both agreed to have meetings and quickly agree to stop fighting. Thailand's Foreign Ministry then released its statement.

The Foreign Ministry confirmed that Trump had talked with Thailand's acting Prime Minister, Phumtham Wechayachai. Trump asked both sides to stop fighting. The ministry posted a statement on social media platform X saying, "Thailand agrees to a ceasefire in general."

The statement also said, "However, Thailand wants Cambodia to show that it truly wants peace."

The Foreign Ministry added that Phumtham asked Trump to tell Cambodia that Thailand wants to have talks soon. These talks would be to plan how to stop fighting and finally solve the conflict peacefully.

The fighting between Thailand and Cambodia near their border has been going on for three days. Because of the fighting, over 150,000 people have had to leave their homes.
