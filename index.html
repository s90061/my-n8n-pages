Trump Says U.S. Sunk Three Ships Off Venezuela

U.S. President Trump said today that the United States has "sunk" a total of three ships near Venezuela. The day before, he had confirmed that the U.S. launched a second deadly attack on suspected drug traffickers in the Caribbean Sea.

According to AFP, Trump told reporters as he left the White House for a state visit to the UK: "We actually sank three ships, not two. You only saw two."

Trump did not give more details about the third ship or if more people died.

On September 15, Trump said U.S. forces hit a second ship in international waters, killing three people. Trump called them "drug trafficker terrorists."

Earlier this month, the U.S. military attacked suspected drug smuggling boats in the Caribbean Sea, and 11 people died. After these attacks, people have often questioned if the Trump government's actions were legal.

The U.S. government released videos of the two previous known actions. They said they had strong evidence that all the people who died were smugglers trying to bring deadly drugs into the U.S.

However, the U.S. did not provide details to support these claims.

These actions by the U.S. military have made the situation more tense in the Caribbean area. The U.S. has sent many navy ships to this area. This has made some people think that Washington might want to remove the government in Venezuela.

Robert Redford Dies; Meryl Streep Calls Him a Superstar, Jane Fonda is Very Sad

Hollywood legend Robert Redford died this morning. Famous actress Meryl Streep, who worked with him in movies like "Out of Africa," honored him, calling him a "superstar." Oscar-winning actress Jane Fonda, who worked with him on many classic films, also shared her sadness. She said he was "a wonderful person in every way."

According to AFP, Meryl Streep released a short statement to the media through her publicist. She said: "One of the great stars has fallen. Rest in peace, my dear friend."

Cindi Berger, Robert Redford's publicist, said in a statement that Robert Redford passed away this morning at his home in Utah. He was 89 years old.

Jane Fonda said: "This morning, I was very shocked to hear that Bob (Robert Redford) had died. I couldn't stop crying. He meant a lot to me and was a wonderful person in every way. He represented an America that we must continue to fight for."

Robert Redford was famous for movies like "Butch Cassidy and the Sundance Kid." When he was young, he was handsome and well-known as a Hollywood golden boy. Later, he also became a successful director and won an Oscar for Best Director.

European Stocks Fall as Markets Wait for Fed Meeting

The U.S. Federal Reserve (Fed) started a two-day meeting today to discuss its money policy. Many people expect the Fed to lower interest rates. Because of this, markets were careful, and the three main European stock indexes all went down today.

The FTSE 100 index in London dropped by 81.37 points, or 0.88%, ending at 9195.66 points.

The DAX index in Frankfurt fell sharply by 419.62 points, or 1.77%, closing at 23329.24 points.

The CAC 40 index in Paris went down by 78.71 points, or 1%, finishing at 7818.22 points.
