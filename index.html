School in Nantou Stops Work and Classes Due to Thunderstorms and Blocked Roads

From last night until early this morning, Nantou County had heavy rain with thunder. The Nantou County Government said that due to mudslides and the only road being blocked, Renai Township's Hukou Elementary School will be closed today for both work and classes.

Heavy Rain in Central and Southern Taiwan: 540 Areas at Risk of Mudslides

Heavy rain continues in central and southern Taiwan. The Ministry of Agriculture's Rural Development and Soil and Water Conservation Agency today (July 31) issued red and yellow warnings for 540 potential mudslide streams.

The agency announced that 338 potential mudslide streams are under a red warning. These are in 29 townships and 115 villages across 6 counties: Nantou, Yunlin, Tainan, Chiayi, Kaohsiung, and Pingtung. Another 202 potential mudslide streams are under a yellow warning. These are in 29 townships and 81 villages across 7 counties: Taichung, Nantou, Yunlin, Tainan, Chiayi, Kaohsiung, and Pingtung.

At the same time, the agency issued red warnings for 8 large-scale land collapse areas. These are in Wanshan Village of Maolin District, Baolai Village of Liugui District in Kaohsiung City; Taihe Village of Meishan Township, Wenfeng Village of Zhuqi Township, Ruili Village of Meishan Township in Chiayi County; Nangang Village of Guoxing Township in Nantou County; Xinglong Village of Liugui District, Baoshan Village of Taoyuan District in Kaohsiung City. Local governments should tell or force people to leave these areas and help them find a safe place to stay.

Taitung's Tuban Village Stops Work and Classes Because Road Is Washed Away

The Taitung County Government announced this morning (July 31) that Tuban Village in Daren Township will stop work and classes today. This is because continuous heavy rain has washed away the base of the East 68 Line, which is the only road leading to the village. Now, only motorcycles can use it. This decision was made to keep people safe when they travel.
