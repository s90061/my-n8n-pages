US military leaders from all over the world met. Trump said America is fighting a "war from within."

Today, hundreds of military leaders from different places around the world met in Virginia, USA. This was a rare meeting. President Trump told these leaders that America is facing a "war from inside the country."

News agencies like AFP and The Hill reported that Trump spoke to the US military leaders at the meeting in Quantico, Virginia. He said, "Together, we bring out the fighting spirit. This spirit has won wars and built this country."

Trump also said, "In the next few years, we will work together to make our military stronger, tougher, faster, braver, and better at fighting than ever before."

He also pointed out that using the National Guard in several American cities to stop problems was important work for some people there. He said this was also a war, a war from inside the country.

Trump also told the US military leaders that if Hamas, a Palestinian group, does not agree to a peace deal for Gaza, they will face serious problems.
