US Treasury Secretary Scott Bessent said that both countries have made a basic agreement for TikTok. TikTok is a popular short video app owned by the Chinese company ByteDance.

Bessent also said that the US and China will probably have more talks about trade and money rules in a few days. He noted that they are also talking about how to work together to stop money laundering and the illegal drug fentanyl in trade.
