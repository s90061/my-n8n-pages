Oil Prices Go Up by More Than 1% Because of Talks Not Working and Strong Demand in the US

Oil prices went up by almost 1 dollar per barrel today. This happened because Russia and Ukraine said the other side was slowing down peace talks. Also, the United States, which uses the most oil in the world, showed strong demand for oil in new reports.

According to Reuters news, Brent crude oil futures went up by 83 cents. This is a 1.2% increase. The price ended at 67.67 US dollars per barrel, which is the highest price in two weeks.

US West Texas Intermediate crude oil futures went up by 81 cents. This is a 1.3% increase. The price ended at 63.52 US dollars per barrel.

US Stock Market Goes Down as People Wait for Fed Leader's Speech

Today, the main stock market indexes in Wall Street went down. Investors are getting ready for an important speech tomorrow morning from Jerome Powell, the head of the Federal Reserve (Fed). They are waiting to hear what he will say about future interest rates in the United States.

The Dow Jones Industrial Average went down by 152.81 points, which is 0.34%. It closed at 44785.50 points.

The S&P 500 index went down by 25.61 points, which is 0.40%. It closed at 6370.17 points.

The Nasdaq index for technology stocks went down by 72.55 points, which is 0.34%. It closed at 21100.31 points.

The Philadelphia Semiconductor index went down by 27.56 points, which is 0.49%. It closed at 5603.26 points.

National Guard in Washington, Trump Says He Will Go on Patrol with Police and Army at Night

US President Trump sent the National Guard to Washington, D.C., the capital city, last week. Today, he said he will go on patrol with the military and police in the streets of Washington tonight.

Reuters news agency reported that Trump spoke on a radio show with Todd Starnes, a reporter from the conservative news channel Newsmax. Trump said, "I think I'll go out tonight with the police, and of course, the military."

Trump used his power as president to send one thousand National Guard members and federal officers to Washington. He said this was because of a lot of violent crime in the city.

Officials in Washington disagreed with Trump. They said that numbers from the government and the city show that violent crime has gone down a lot after it went up in 2023.

Yesterday, US Vice President JD Vance and Defense Secretary Pete Hegseth looked at the National Guard at Union Station in Washington. People protesting there shouted and laughed at them.
