Taiwan Stock Market Goes Up by 162.37 Points

The Taiwan stock market closed today with an increase of 162.37 points. The final score was 25982.91 points. This is a 0.63% rise. The total money traded was 455.714 billion New Taiwan dollars.

The TAIEX index started at 26078.29 points. During the day, it reached a high of 26325.79 points and a low of 25982.91 points. The index without financial stocks went up by 162.71 points, reaching 22866.76 points.

Here are the changes for the eight main types of stocks: Ceramics and bricks stocks went down by 0.61%. Food stocks went down by 0.29%. Plastics and chemicals stocks went up by 0.59%. Textile stocks went down by 0.45%. Electrical and mechanical stocks went up by 0.89%. Paper stocks went down by 0.22%. Construction stocks went down by 0.58%. Financial stocks went down by 0.17%.

The number of buy orders was 20,725,201 units. The number of sell orders was 11,529,973 units. The number of traded units was 7,609,091 units.

At the end of trading, 338 companies saw their stock prices go up. 603 companies saw their stock prices go down. 129 companies stayed the same.

The top 5 most traded stocks were Powerchip Semiconductor, Winbond Electronics, Wistron, Taiwan Glass, and Innolux.

The top 5 stocks with the biggest increases were Tainan Enterprises, Apex International, World Gym-KY, Teco Electric and Machinery, and Powerchip Semiconductor.

The top 5 stocks with the biggest decreases were ZuoFu Technology, DaMing, Zhongtai, First Copper, and Pertem.

Taiwan Nichia Promotes Dai Chen-chia to Executive Director, He Becomes the Highest-Ranking Taiwanese Manager

Taiwan Nichia announced today that Mr. Dai Chen-chia, who was a Managing Director, has been promoted. He is now an Executive Director. This makes him the highest-ranking Taiwanese manager in the Nichia group. Mr. Dai Chen-chia is now the third most important person at Taiwan Nichia, after the President and Vice President.

Taiwan Nichia explained in a press release that the position of Executive Director is very important in Japanese companies. It is just below the President and Vice President. This person is in charge of managing the main business and making important plans. They also play a key role in board meetings and regular meetings. This new appointment shows that Nichia highly values Mr. Dai Chen-chia's leadership and contributions over many years.

Besides his work at Nichia, Mr. Dai Chen-chia is also the Vice Chairman of Formosa Epitaxy, a subsidiary company of Nichia. This helps to make their connection in the semiconductor industry stronger.

Mr. Li Kuo-guang, the Chairman of Formosa Epitaxy, said that it is very special for a high-level Taiwanese manager to become an Executive Director in a Japanese company. This shows that Taiwanese professionals are very competitive in international companies. It also means that economic exchange and trust between Taiwan and Japan are growing deeper.

The title "Executive Director" is like an Executive Director in a Taiwanese company. In Japanese companies, this role often helps the President and manages all company matters.

New Taipei Woman Beat Her Partner and Burned Her Face, Leading to Death; She Had Also Hurt Her 4 Years Ago

In February this year, a woman named Luo from New Taipei City used a billiard cue to hit her partner's head. She also used a lighter to burn her partner's face, causing her death. The New Taipei District Prosecutors Office has charged Ms. Luo with causing death by injury. In court, Ms. Luo admitted to all the bad things she did. Investigations show that Ms. Luo had hurt the victim, Ms. Qiu, four years ago. This was not the first time she was violent.

Ms. Luo, who is 34 years old, and Ms. Qiu were partners. They lived together in a rented apartment in Xinzhuang District, New Taipei City. On February 4th this year, around 1 AM, Ms. Luo first pushed Ms. Qiu with her hands. Ms. Qiu fell and hit her head on the ground. After Ms. Qiu became unconscious, Ms. Luo moved her to the bed. While moving her, Ms. Qiu's head hit something again.

Ms. Luo then used a billiard cue to hit Ms. Qiu's head and body. After that, she used a lighter to light tissue paper and other things that burn easily. She used the fire to burn Ms. Qiu's face, head, and shoulders. This caused a lot of bleeding at the back of Ms. Qiu's head and a broken bone (occipital bone) on the left side of her head. In the end, Ms. Qiu died from brain damage.

The New Taipei District Prosecutors Office recently charged Ms. Luo with causing death by injury. Yesterday, the New Taipei District Court held a hearing to prepare for the trial. Ms. Luo admitted to all the crimes mentioned above.

Also, investigations showed that Ms. Luo had hurt Ms. Qiu many years ago. In February 2021, the two had an argument. Ms. Luo used a wine bottle and an ashtray to hit Ms. Qiu. She also grabbed Ms. Qiu's head and hit it against a wall. This caused injuries to Ms. Qiu's head. At that time, the prosecutor charged Ms. Luo with assault. However, the charges were dropped after both women reached an agreement.
