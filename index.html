Trump to Meet Putin; US, UK, EU, Ukraine Officials to Talk First

A spokesperson for the British Prime Minister's office said that British Foreign Secretary David Lammy and US Vice President JD Vance will meet in the UK today. They will talk with friends from Ukraine and Europe. The meeting is to discuss what US President Trump wants to do for peace in Ukraine.

Reuters reported that a Downing Street spokesperson also said that before this security meeting, British Prime Minister Keir Starmer spoke with Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelenskyy. They talked about Donald Trump's ideas to end the war between Russia and Ukraine.

Trump announced yesterday that he will meet with Russian President Vladimir Putin on August 15 in Alaska, USA.

The British Prime Minister's office spokesperson said: "The Prime Minister spoke with President Zelenskyy this morning. They are looking forward to a meeting today with national security advisors from Europe, Ukraine, and the United States. The British Foreign Secretary and the US Vice President will lead this meeting."

The spokesperson also added that both Starmer and Zelenskyy "agreed that this meeting is very important to discuss how to achieve a fair and lasting peace."

Poland's President Nawrocki to Visit US in Early September, Will Meet Trump

An official from the office of Poland's new President, Karol Nawrocki, said today that US President Donald Trump has invited Nawrocki to Washington in early September.

Reuters reported that Nawrocki became the President of Poland on August 6. He has often said that having a good relationship between Poland and the United States is very important.

During his election campaign, Nawrocki had the support of Poland's main nationalist opposition party, the "Law and Justice Party." He also met with Trump in the Oval Office before the Polish election in May, and Trump supported his election.

Pawel Szefernaker, an official from the Polish President's office, wrote on X (a social media platform): "In an official letter on his inauguration day, US President Trump invited Polish President Nawrocki to the White House for an official work meeting on September 3, 2025."
