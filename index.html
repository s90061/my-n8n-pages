Taiwan Stock Market Goes Up by 162.37 Points

Taipei's stock market closed today with a rise of 162.37 points. The final number was 25,982.91 points. This is a 0.63% increase. The total money traded was 455.714 billion New Taiwan Dollars.

The market started at 26,078.29 points. During the day, it went as high as 26,325.79 points and as low as 25,982.91 points. The index without financial stocks was 22,866.76 points, rising by 162.71 points.

Here is how the eight main types of stocks changed: Cement and Ceramics stocks fell by 0.61%, Food stocks fell by 0.29%, Plastic and Chemical stocks rose by 0.59%, Textile stocks fell by 0.45%, Electrical and Mechanical stocks rose by 0.89%, Paper stocks fell by 0.22%, Construction stocks fell by 0.58%, and Financial stocks fell by 0.17%.

The number of buy orders was 20,725,201 shares. The number of sell orders was 11,529,973 shares. The total number of shares traded was 7,609,091 shares.

When the market closed, 338 companies saw their stock prices go up, 603 companies saw their stock prices go down, and 129 companies had no change.

The top 5 most traded stocks were Powerchip Semiconductor, Winbond Electronics, Wistron, Taiwan Glass, and Innolux.

The top 5 stocks that rose the most were Tainan, Apex Precision Technology, World Gym-KY, TECO, and Powerchip Semiconductor.

The top 5 stocks that fell the most were Zonfotech, Darfon, Chung Tai, First Copper, and Protech.

Taiwan Nichia Company Promotes Dai Zhen-jia to Senior Managing Director, Making Him the Highest-Ranking Taiwanese Manager

Taiwan Nichia (Nichia) announced today that Mr. Dai Zhen-jia, who is currently a managing director, has been promoted. He is now a Senior Managing Director. This makes him the Taiwanese manager with the highest position in the Nichia group. Mr. Dai Zhen-jia is now the third most important person at Taiwan Nichia, after the President and Vice President.

Taiwan Nichia explained in a press release that the position of Senior Managing Director is very important in Japanese companies. It is only below the President and Vice President. This person is in charge of the main business operations and making big plans. They also play a very important role in board meetings and regular meetings. This new appointment shows that Nichia highly values Mr. Dai Zhen-jia's leadership and hard work over many years.

Besides his role at Nichia, Mr. Dai Zhen-jia also works as the Vice Chairman of Taya Semiconductor. Taya Semiconductor is a company owned by Nichia. This shows a deeper plan to combine businesses in the semiconductor industry.

Mr. Li Guo-guang, the Chairman of Taya, said that a high-level Taiwanese manager being promoted to Senior Managing Director in a Japanese company is very special in the history of Japanese business. It shows that skilled people from Taiwan are very competitive in international companies. It also means that the economic and cultural trust between Taiwan and Japan is growing stronger.

A "Senior Managing Director" is similar to an Executive Director in Taiwanese companies. In Japanese companies, this person often helps the President and manages all company matters.

Woman in New Taipei Beats Her Partner to Death and Burned Her Face; She Also Hurt Her Four Years Ago

In February this year, a woman named Luo from New Taipei City used a pool cue to hit her girlfriend's head and a lighter to burn her face. This caused her girlfriend's death. The New Taipei District Prosecutors Office has charged Ms. Luo with causing death by injury. Ms. Luo admitted to all the charges in court. It was found that Ms. Luo had also hurt Ms. Qiu four years ago, so this was not the first time she was violent.

Ms. Luo, who is 34 years old, and Ms. Qiu were partners. They lived together in a rented apartment in Xinzhuang District, New Taipei City. Around 1 AM on February 4th this year, Ms. Luo first pushed Ms. Qiu with her hands, causing Ms. Qiu's head to hit the ground. After Ms. Qiu lost consciousness, Ms. Luo moved her to the bed, but Ms. Qiu's head hit something again.

Ms. Luo also used a pool cue to hit Ms. Qiu's head and body. Then, she used a lighter to set fire to tissues and other things that burn easily. She burned Ms. Qiu's face, head, and shoulders. This caused a lot of bleeding at the back of Ms. Qiu's head and a broken bone (left occipital bone). In the end, Ms. Qiu died from brain damage.

The New Taipei District Prosecutors Office recently charged Ms. Luo with causing death by injury. The New Taipei District Court held a preparatory hearing yesterday, and Ms. Luo admitted to all the crimes mentioned.

Also, it was found that Ms. Luo had hurt Ms. Qiu many years ago. In February 2021, the two argued. Ms. Luo used a wine bottle and an ashtray to hit Ms. Qiu. She also grabbed Ms. Qiu's head and hit it against a wall. This caused injuries to Ms. Qiu's head. At that time, the prosecutor charged Ms. Luo with assault. However, they reached an agreement, and the charges were dropped.
