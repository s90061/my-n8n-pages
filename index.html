Fed Rate Cut Expectations Rise, European Stocks Close Higher

London, August 13 (CNA) - New inflation numbers came out from the United States yesterday. These numbers did not change much. This made investors think that the US Federal Reserve (Fed) will lower interest rates. Because of this, the main stock markets in Europe closed higher today.

The FTSE 100 index in London went up by 17.42 points, or 0.19%. It closed at 9165.23 points.

The DAX index in Frankfurt went up by 160.81 points, or 0.67%. It closed at 24185.59 points.

The CAC 40 index in Paris went up by 51.55 points, or 0.66%. It closed at 7804.97 points.

Typhoon Yangliu Weakens to a Mild Typhoon, Taiwan Island Leaves the Storm Circle

Taipei, August 13 (CNA) - Typhoon Yangliu is moving away. The weather agency said that Yangliu's strength has become weaker. At 11 PM, it weakened into a mild typhoon. Taiwan island is now out of the storm's circle. The land warning is only for Penghu and Kinmen now. Kinmen, in particular, still needs to watch out for strong wind and rain until early tomorrow morning.

According to the Central Weather Administration, Typhoon Yangliu's center at 11 PM today was at 23.7 degrees North and 118.0 degrees East. This is about 90 kilometers southwest of Kinmen. It is moving northwest at a speed of 30 to 33 kilometers per hour.

The weather agency said that Yangliu's strength became weaker in the last 3 hours. Its center is now near the sea southwest of Kinmen. It will move northwest. Taiwan island has left its storm circle. It still affects Penghu and Kinmen, with wind and rain continuing. It is expected that this typhoon's strength will continue to get weaker, and its storm circle will also get smaller.

Weather forecaster Chu Mei-lin said in a press conference that Yangliu is moving northwest quickly. It is expected to reach China late tonight. Its strength will also slowly get weaker. Taiwan island is now out of the storm's circle. The land warning is only for Penghu and Kinmen. Kinmen still needs to watch out for strong wind and rain from tonight until early tomorrow morning.

Chu Mei-lin said that the eastern part of Taiwan has winds from the south-southeast. So, Hualien and Taitung will still have heavy rain until tomorrow morning. Many strong rain clouds are forming on the east and south sides of Yangliu. Because of this, the southwestern coast and Penghu will still have rain that stops and starts.

Chu Mei-lin pointed out that after tomorrow morning, the rain will become less, and the weather will become stable. Most places will be partly cloudy to sunny. The western part of Taiwan needs to watch out for thunderstorms in the afternoon, especially the central mountains where there will be heavier rain. The eastern part will have rain that stops and starts.

Chu Mei-lin said that the weather will be mostly stable until August 17. Starting from the evening of August 16, there will be more rain due to moisture from the south. On August 19 and 20, the weather will go back to being partly cloudy to sunny.

According to the weather agency, from 12 AM on August 12 to 11 PM on August 13, the highest rainfall was 453.5 millimeters in Dahan Mountain, Pingtung County, 381.5 millimeters in Fengnan, Hualien County, 351.5 millimeters in Chishang, Taitung County, and 250.5 millimeters in Nantianchi, Kaohsiung City. The areas with the strongest winds were Orchid Island and Green Island (above level 17), Dongji Island (level 13), Taitung (level 12), Chenggong and New Taipei (level 11), Xinwu, Su-ao, Pengjia Islet, Taipei, and Sun Moon Lake (level 10).
