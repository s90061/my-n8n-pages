Wildfire on Arthur's Seat, a Famous Place in Edinburgh; Smoke Goes High into the Sky

A wildfire started today at Arthur's Seat, a well-known old volcano in Edinburgh, Scotland. The fire spread quickly. People could see smoke going up into the sky from many kilometers away. Arthur's Seat is a popular place for tourists.

Arthur's Seat is 250 meters high and is the highest point in Holyrood Park. It is a very good place to see Edinburgh from above. It is near the Scottish Parliament and the end of the famous Royal Mile street.

The Scottish Fire and Rescue Service said on X (a social media platform): "Our team members are working to put out a fire in the open area of Arthur's Seat in Holyrood Park, Edinburgh."

The police told people walking and driving to stay away from the area.

August is a very busy time for tourists in Edinburgh. There are the Edinburgh Art Festival and the Edinburgh Festival Fringe. Also, the British rock band Oasis, who have not played together for many years, are having a concert there.

Delta Air Lines Plane's Wing Hit Another Plane Before Taking Off; All 198 People on Board are Safe

Delta Air Lines, an American airline, said that one of its planes hit another plane today. This happened in Atlanta when the plane was moving backward from the gate. Delta Air Lines said that all 198 people on the plane were safe.

Delta Air Lines told The Associated Press in a short statement that the wing of their plane touched another plane that had no one inside.

The flight was planned to go from Atlanta to Guatemala City. Because of the accident, the trip was delayed. The passengers then got on a different plane. This information is from FlightAware, a website that tracks flights.

At the time of the accident, there were 192 passengers, two pilots, and four flight attendants on the plane. This makes a total of 198 people. Delta Air Lines said that no one was hurt.

Atlanta News First, a local TV station, said that Delta Air Lines flight DL1830 hit an Airbus A350 airplane.

This flight later took off at 1:20 PM local time.

Trump and Putin to Talk About Russia-Ukraine War; German Leader Thinks Zelenskyy Will Be There

US President Trump will meet with Russian President Putin on August 15th to talk about ending the war between Russia and Ukraine. Today, the German Chancellor, Mr. Merz, said that Germany expects the Ukrainian President, Mr. Zelenskyy, to be at this important meeting.

The AFP news agency reported that Putin and Trump will meet in Alaska, USA. They will try to solve the conflict between Russia and Ukraine, which has been going on for more than three years. Ukraine and European countries say that the Ukrainian government in Kyiv must be part of these talks.

Mr. Merz spoke on German TV (ARD). He said, "We hope and expect that the Ukrainian government, President Zelenskyy, will be part of this meeting."

Mr. Merz told ARD that Germany is working closely with the US government in Washington to make sure President Zelenskyy is there for the talks.

He also said: "We cannot agree to a situation where Russia and the United States talk about, or even decide, land issues without Europeans and Ukrainians. I believe the US government thinks the same way as us."

Mr. Merz said he hopes these talks will lead to important progress towards a peaceful solution, maybe even a "breakthrough."

He said, "We hope for a big step forward on August 15th. Most importantly, we want a ceasefire to happen and peace talks to begin in Ukraine."

Russia and Ukraine have had three rounds of talks this year, but they have not reached a ceasefire.
