Wildfire at Edinburgh's Arthur's Seat Sends Smoke High into the Sky

A wildfire started and spread quickly at Arthur's Seat, a famous old volcano in Edinburgh, Scotland. People could see smoke rising high into the sky from many kilometers away.

Arthur's Seat is 250 meters high and is the tallest point in Holyrood Park. It offers great views of Edinburgh. It is also near the Scottish Parliament and the Royal Mile.

The Scottish Fire and Rescue Service said on X (formerly Twitter) that their team was dealing with a fire in the open area of Arthur's Seat in Holyrood Park, Edinburgh.

The police asked people walking and driving to avoid the area.

August is a busy month for tourism in Edinburgh. The Edinburgh Art Festival and Edinburgh Festival Fringe are happening. Also, the British rock band Oasis, who are back together after many years, is holding a concert there.

Delta Plane's Wing Hit Another Plane Before Takeoff; All 198 People Safe

Delta Air Lines said that one of its planes hit another plane while it was moving backward from the gate in Atlanta this morning. Delta said that all 198 people on board were safe.

Delta Air Lines told the Associated Press in a short statement that the wing of the plane touched another empty plane.

According to FlightAware, a website that tracks flights, the flight was supposed to go from Atlanta to Guatemala City, the capital of Guatemala. The passengers whose trip was delayed later got on another plane.

At the time of the event, there were 192 passengers, two pilots, and four flight attendants, making a total of 198 people. Delta Air Lines said no one was reported hurt.

Atlanta News First, a local TV station, reported that Delta Air Lines flight DL1830 hit an Airbus A350 plane.

The flight later took off at 1:20 PM local time.

Trump and Putin to Discuss Ukraine War; German Leader Expects Zelenskyy to Attend

US President Trump will meet with Russian President Putin on August 15 to talk about ending the war between Russia and Ukraine. Germany's Chancellor Merz said today that Germany believes Ukrainian President Zelenskyy will be at this meeting.

Agence France-Presse (AFP) reported that Putin and Trump will meet in Alaska, USA. They will try to solve the conflict between Russia and Ukraine, which has lasted over three years. Ukraine and Europe say that Kyiv must be part of the talks.

Merz said in an interview with German TV channel ARD, "We hope and expect that the Ukrainian government, President Zelenskyy, will be part of this meeting."

Merz told ARD that Berlin is working closely with Washington to make sure Zelenskyy attends the talks.

He said, "We cannot accept that Russia and the United States talk about or even decide on land issues without including Europeans and Ukrainians. I believe the US government has the same opinion as us."

Merz said he hopes these talks will lead to important progress towards a peaceful solution, or even a "breakthrough."

He said, "We hope for a breakthrough on August 15. Most importantly, we hope for a ceasefire and peace talks in Ukraine."

Russia and Ukraine have had three rounds of talks this year, but they have not reached a ceasefire.
