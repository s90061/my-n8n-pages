Pop Music Star Taylor Swift Announces Engagement to Football Player Boyfriend on Social Media

American pop star Taylor Swift shared happy news today. She and Travis Kelce, a football player for the NFL Chiefs team, announced their engagement on social media. They have been together for over two years.

Reuters reported that the couple wrote together in an Instagram post: "Your English teacher and your sports teacher are getting married!" The post also included a picture of Kelce asking Swift to marry him.

The engagement post quickly received more than 1.8 million likes in less than 20 minutes. The NFL also sent congratulations to the couple.

Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce have been a couple for over two years. They started dating after Swift performed at the Chiefs' home stadium, Arrowhead Stadium, in 2023. However, Kelce had shown interest in Swift as early as 2016.

French Political Problems Cause Uncertainty, European Stocks Go Down

Investors are worried that France will have more political problems. This makes the market unsure. Because of this, the three main European stock indexes closed lower today. Bank stocks traded in Paris also went down.

The FTSE 100 index in London fell by 55.60 points, which is 0.60%. It closed at 9265.80 points.

The DAX index in Frankfurt went down by 120.25 points, or 0.50%. It closed at 24152.87 points.

The CAC 40 index in Paris dropped a lot, losing 133.23 points, or 1.70%. It closed at 7709.81 points.

Zhubei Air Smells Like Burning Plastic; Environmental Bureau Investigating Possible Leak

The Hsinchu County Environmental Protection Bureau (EPB) announced tonight that there is a smell of burning plastic in the air in many parts of Zhubei City. They first thought it was not open burning. It seems more likely that a strange-smelling substance has leaked out. The EPB will continue to search for where the pollution is coming from.

The Hsinchu County EPB said in a statement that they have checked many areas. These include different sections of Guangming 6th Road, Zhuangjing North and South Roads, Jiafeng North and South Roads, the area around the High-Speed Rail Station, different sections of Dongxing Road, and Shixing Road. They have not found any clear source of the pollution yet.

The EPB explained that they looked at the results from their sensors. The sensor data first showed that it was not like open burning. It looked more like a leak of a strange substance. However, the sensors did not show clear changes over time and in different places. This means they cannot use the sensor results to find the source of the pollution. They will continue to investigate.
