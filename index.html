Asian Soft Tennis Championship: Yu Kai-wen and Huang Shih-yuan Win Gold in Mixed Doubles

Taiwanese players have done very well at the Asian Soft Tennis Championship in Korea. Yu Kai-wen and Huang Shih-yuan played together in the mixed doubles. On September 18, they won the gold medal. This is the first time in 17 years that Taiwan's soft tennis team has won a gold medal at this championship.

The 9th Asian Soft Tennis Championship is happening in Mungyeong, Korea. Yesterday, Chen Po-yi won a silver medal in men's singles. Today, Yu Kai-wen and Huang Shih-yuan won another medal, a gold in mixed doubles. The Minister of Sports, Li Yang, sent them a message to say congratulations. He hopes the Taiwanese soft tennis players will continue to work hard and get the best results.

Today, Yu Kai-wen and Huang Shih-yuan played very well in the mixed doubles. In the semi-finals, they played against a strong Japanese team. This Japanese team had won three world championship gold medals. But Yu Kai-wen stopped the strong attacks from the Japanese player, and Huang Shih-yuan played well from the back of the court. They won that match 5-2 and went to the final.

In the final, they played another Japanese team. Yu Kai-wen and Huang Shih-yuan had good teamwork again. They started well and quickly led 3-0. Even when the other team tried to catch up, the Taiwanese team stayed calm in a very important game, the sixth game. They won that game and then won the match 5-2. This was Taiwan's first gold medal at this Asian Soft Tennis Championship.

Zhu Wen-qing, the head of the Soft Tennis Association, said this mixed doubles gold medal made the whole team feel very happy. It also ended Taiwan's 17-year wait for a gold medal at the Asian Championship. He hopes the new generation of players will do even better. He also hopes they will aim for the 2026 Nagoya Asian Games and win more honors for Taiwan.

Taipei Auto Show Starts December 31, Focusing on Electric Cars and Smart Green Energy

The Taipei New Car and New Energy Vehicle Exhibition will start on December 31. The show will feature cars that use both gasoline and electricity, and fully electric cars. Many car companies from Taiwan and other countries will show their new cars. These include expensive cars, fast cars, electric cars, and older, classic cars. People who visit can learn about smart technology and how cars are becoming more eco-friendly.

The Taipei Automobile Dealers Association is organizing the show. It will be from December 31 to January 4, 2026. The Association said today in a press release that big car brands will join the show. They will show cars that use hybrid power (HEV/PHEV) and full electric power (BEV). This shows how car technology is improving, how far electric cars can go, and how easy it is to charge them.

The Association also said that the car show will bring together many car brands from Taiwan and other countries. There will be luxury cars, performance cars, electric cars, and classic cars. Many new car models will be shown for the first time. They will show the latest designs, technology, and driving experience.

There will also be a special area called the "Mobility Zone." In this area, visitors can see car chargers, useful car accessories, camper vans, and special films for cars. This will help visitors understand new ways of moving around.

The Association explained that visitors can also try smart driving, advanced charging systems, ways to reduce carbon, car internet, and online software updates. The show will also have talks about "Net Zero Mobility." These talks will share ideas about new low-carbon technology, government support, and challenges for the car industry. They will discuss the future of smart transportation and reducing carbon. This will help visitors understand new smart green energy technology and how the industry is changing.

Jimmy Kimmel's Show Stops After Comments on Kirk Shooting, Hollywood Supports Him

The TV channel ABC has stopped Jimmy Kimmel's late-night talk show. This happened after he spoke about the shooting of Charlie Kirk. Kirk was a conservative political figure. Today, Hollywood people are supporting Kimmel. They are also saying that the White House is trying to stop people from speaking freely.

ABC, which belongs to Disney, said that "Jimmy Kimmel Live!" will not be on TV for an unknown time. This is because at least one TV station did not want to show it. Also, the main TV regulator threatened to look into Kimmel's comments.

Reuters news agency said that Jimmy Kimmel has often criticized former President Trump. On his show on September 15, he talked about Charlie Kirk being killed. Kimmel said that Kirk's friends were using his death to get political advantages.

The Writers Guild and the Screen Actors Guild said that stopping Kimmel's show because of his comments is against the right to speak freely. This right is in the constitution. They also said that ABC should not let the government make them do this.

This is another important event after other media people, university staff, teachers, and business workers faced problems for talking about Kirk's death.

Kirk was killed last week in Utah. He was 31 years old. A 22-year-old person has been accused of the crime. However, nobody knows why the crime happened yet.
