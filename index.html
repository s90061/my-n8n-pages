Boy Rescued in Mountain Accident; Grandfather and Female Hiker Die

In Xinyi Township, Nantou County, a mountain accident happened today on Mount Kekean. A 70-year-old man and a 63-year-old female hiker had no signs of life when they were rescued and brought down the mountain. The 8-year-old boy with them showed signs of hypothermia but was stable. He is now in the hospital for treatment and observation.

The Nantou County Fire Department received a call at noon today. The 8-year-old boy, his grandfather, and another female hiker started their climb on August 8th from Hualien County. They planned to hike the Southern Third Section and expected to come down at Dongpu, Xinyi Township, Nantou County, on August 17th. They met a typhoon during their trip, which caused them to be cold for a long time. It is believed that they lost strength because of this. The fire department thought the patients' lives were in danger, and the place was far away, so they immediately asked for a helicopter to help.

The Air Force team sent a helicopter, which arrived at Mount Kekean in the afternoon. They landed and helped the old man, the female hiker, and the boy come down the mountain. Later, they were taken to the hospital by ambulance. However, the old man and the female hiker had no signs of life before reaching the hospital and died even after rescue efforts. The boy had symptoms of hypothermia but was stable. The full details of the accident are still being investigated.

The Fire Department asks people to be very ready before going to the mountains, as the weather can change very fast. They suggest checking your own abilities and not trying to climb routes that are too hard for you. Also, carry satellite communication devices. These can quickly give your location in an emergency, saving important time for rescue.

French, British, German Leaders to Hold Ukraine Allies Meeting on 17th to Discuss Peace Steps

The French President's office announced that the leaders of France, the United Kingdom, and Germany will host an online meeting tomorrow. This meeting will include the "Coalition of the Willing," which is made up of Kyiv's allies. They will talk about steps to bring peace to Ukraine.

According to Agence France-Presse, the French President's office said that this meeting, led by French President Emmanuel Macron, British Prime Minister Keir Starmer, and German Chancellor Friedrich Merz, is happening for two reasons. First, the leaders of the United States and Russia had a big meeting yesterday. Second, Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelenskyy will go to Washington D.C. on August 18th to talk with U.S. President Donald Trump.

After talking with Russian President Vladimir Putin yesterday, Trump said at a joint press conference that both sides agreed on many topics but had not yet made a deal.

Later, Trump was interviewed by Fox News. He added that he and Putin agreed that the Russia-Ukraine war could end with an exchange of land and some kind of security promise from the United States.
